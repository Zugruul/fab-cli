# Wind Cutter
House Miharu has forged Misteria's finest bracers for generations, weaving honor into every stitch. Shaped by the grace and strength of mountain winds, their designs channel the ridgeline's resolve; here, the crimson threads have been braided in the old ways, binding martial prowess to steel. Sleeve them and feel tradition settle at your wrists, your chi aligned with mountain, mist, and clan.
