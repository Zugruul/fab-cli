# Trench Of Watery Depths

They say flamboyant Captain Rue's trench coat drifts the Reach, untouched by salt or time, waiting for any pirate bold enough to claim it. Button it to feel its eldritch weave resize to fit, the arcane hum of the ancient [Dhani](~DhaniEmpire) at your shoulders. Then rise with aplomb from the watery grave with booty thought long lost at sea.
