# Bolt'N' Boots
Out on the frontier, an unknown tinkerer wrought iron and supple leather to steady a lone Ranger's stance as they faced down the big city scrappers. How they wandered into Blackjack's is a tale for another season. Let's just say the grit in their seams remains, and there's grim purpose in every rivet. In boots like these, you can stand tall and be a law unto yourself.
