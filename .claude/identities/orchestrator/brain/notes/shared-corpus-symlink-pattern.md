Knowledge shared verbatim across role brains lives as ONE physical copy (card-vault) with relative symlinks in the other brains — desync becomes impossible by construction, git tracks the links as mode-120000 objects, and brain.py reads through them transparently. The one hazard: writing through a symlink edits the shared file, so mint/hand-edit over shared slugs is forbidden outside the owning role's editorial process; a hash manifest catches unauthorized changes ([[template-checker-manifest-pattern]]).
