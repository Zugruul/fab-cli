Official zone layout (TRP 5.6, mandatory at comp/pro REL), from the player's perspective: hero center with weapon zones adjacent and arsenal below; deck column on one side (graveyard above deck, pitch toward center, banished below deck); equipment column opposite (head above chest, arms toward center, legs below); combat chain shared equally, chain links grouped and ordered across the table; permanents on the owner's side below the chain, tapped = rotated ~90°; sub-cards clearly associated; inventory + out-of-game objects OUTSIDE the play-space. Deviations need judge approval; Head Judge is final authority. Card arrangement rules: only deck + combat chain are strictly ordered; players may rearrange their OWN graveyard/banished for communication/efficiency (Phoenix Flame pile, decompose sorting) but obsessive sorting to TRACK DECK CONTENTS = note-taking infraction; extra piles allowed per distinct continuous effect on face-down cards (intimidated card separated); playable-from-zone cards may be fanned; never rearrange zones you don't own without permission.
