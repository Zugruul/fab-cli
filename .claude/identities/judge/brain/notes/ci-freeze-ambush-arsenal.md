**Cold Snap** (Ice Action): "Target hero may pay {r}{r}{r}. If they don't, freeze a card in their arsenal or an ally they control until the start of your next turn." **Stadium Security** (Revered Action Attack): "While this is in your arsenal, if you've controlled a Toughness token this turn, this gets ambush." Ambush (CR 8.3.28): "While this is in your arsenal, you may defend with it."

Freeze (CR 8.5.34): "To freeze an object, that object cannot be played and its abilities cannot be activated for the duration of the effect."

Ruling: freeze's definition explicitly restricts PLAYING the object and ACTIVATING its abilities — it does not mention defending. Defending with a card is the discrete "Add (defend)" effect (CR 8.5.32: "to add a card to a chain link as a defending card, move it from its current zone onto that chain link"), which is neither "playing" the card nor "activating an ability" in the CR's technical sense. Therefore a frozen Stadium Security sitting in arsenal with ambush active CAN still be added as a defending card straight from the arsenal — freeze does not stop ambush-defense. Only actually PLAYING the frozen card from arsenal, or activating one of its activated abilities, is illegal while frozen.

See [[kw-freeze]], [[kw-ambush]].
