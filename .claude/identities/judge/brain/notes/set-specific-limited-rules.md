Set-specific limited rules (TRP A.7 — CHANGES PER SET, re-check the vendored TRP after each release): competitive boosters = 16 cards, 14-15 limited-pool-legal + 1-2 extra cards excluded/non-draftable (extras vary: cold-foils, legendary/fabled/marvel rarity, extended-art, micro-text, expansion cards — set-dependent). Sealed pack counts vary by set: 6 packs / 30+ deck (WTR era through Heavy Hitters), 4 packs (Bright Lights), 8 packs / exactly 30 (Part the Mistveil onward incl. Omens of the Third Age). Set macros/rules: Uprising max 3 Phoenix Flame per pool; Rosetta starts with Sanctuary of Aria macro; High Seas starts with Treasure Island macro; Omens of the Third Age starts with Omens of Arcana macro; Bright Lights crack-shuffle also strips mid-pack arena cards. When judging limited, ALWAYS check this table for the actual set in the vendored TRP (A.7). Links: [[limited-format-procedures]], [[limited-product-handling]], [[swiss-rounds-and-time-limits]].
