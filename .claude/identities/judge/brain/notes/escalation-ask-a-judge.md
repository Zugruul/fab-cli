Escalation ladder when a question isn't settled on the table:
1. Comprehensive Rules for interactions, TRP for tournament policy, PPG for penalties (all rules.fabtcg.com/txt/latest, 2026-06-10) — cite the section.
2. The Head Judge is the final on-site authority and the only one who may deviate — see [[head-judge-authority]] and [[deviations]]. Appeals go to the HJ per [[judge-calls-and-appeals]].
3. When the official documents genuinely don't resolve the question (novel interaction, ambiguous card text after checking [[card-text-from-json]]), take it to the FAB judge community:
   Discord #ask-a-judge — https://discord.com/channels/874145774135558164/1020649907314495528

Do not fabricate a ruling to fill a gap. If unresolved, say so, apply the most principled reading via [[ppg-philosophy]] (education, equity, mission), and flag it for the Head Judge / community. Legality questions are always live-fetch: [[card-legality-live-fetch]]. — judge Discord.
