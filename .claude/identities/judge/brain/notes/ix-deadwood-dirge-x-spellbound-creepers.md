VERIFIED INTERACTION (CR build 2026-06-10). Deadwood Dirge (red, Runeblade Action, 0-cost: destroy an aura you control → if you do, create 3 Runechant tokens; go again) × Spellbound Creepers (Legs: Once per Turn Instant — {r}, bind counter: play your next non-attack action card this turn as though it were an instant; activate only if you've attacked or defended with an attack action card this turn) × [[kw-token-runechant]] (CR 8.6.3: "When you play an attack action card or activate a weapon attack, destroy this and deal 1 arcane damage to target opposing hero"). CORE LINE: with Creepers pre-activated, play an attack → all Runechant triggers stack ABOVE the attack (6.6.6) → respond with Dirge as an instant, destroying a Runechant whose trigger is pending. The destroyed Runechant's trigger STILL DEALS its 1 arcane damage: the triggered-layer is source-independent, Runechant is an EVENT trigger so the 5.3.2a state-fizzle check doesn't apply; "destroy this" fails (1.8.9, object gone) but remaining effects continue (5.3.4b). Net: zero damage lost, 1 spent Runechant → 3 fresh ones. NEW TOKENS DON'T TRIGGER for the current attack — they entered after the play event; they wait for the next attack/weapon activation. Dirge's go again still grants an action point on resolution (5.3.5). FIRST-ATTACK TRAP: in the trigger window of your FIRST attack this turn, Creepers is NOT yet activatable — an attack only "becomes attacking" when it moves onto the combat chain in the Attack Step (7.2.2a), AFTER the Runechant triggers above it resolve; "this turn" on Creepers' effect means no carry-over from a prior turn. Works from the SECOND attack onward (activate Creepers at any priority window after attack #1). Weapon attacks trigger Runechants and the combo responds to those too, but a weapon activation itself never satisfies Creepers' condition (not an attack action card). Destroying a non-Runechant aura instead: pending triggers resolve normally, 3 tokens still created. OPPONENT'S TURN: defending with an attack action card enables Creepers → Dirge at instant speed during a reaction window as pure setup (Runechants only trigger off YOUR plays). Card Vault lists no contradicting rulings_errata.
