Monarch (legal 2021-05-07, 306 cards) release-notes rulings — OLD PAGE, verified against CR 2026-06. Introduced: Light/Shadow talents (Light Warrior hero plays Generic/Light/Warrior/Light-Warrior; plain Warrior can't play Light Warrior); hero's soul (public, face-up under hero); Instant–Auras; [[kw-ally]] (attack once/turn for an AP; can be attacked; not heroes); [[kw-mentor]] — SUPERSEDED: the mentor ABILITY keyword was replaced by the Mentor TYPE in 2022 (CR 8.3.12 note); charge (optional additional cost, max one per card, card can't charge itself; only charge-keyword placement = "charged" — cf. Spirit of Eirina in [[release-notes-dynasty]]); [[kw-blood-debt]] (lose 1{h} at beginning of YOUR end phase per face-up blood-debt card banished); [[kw-spellvoid]] (replacement, optional, single source, no response window, no leftover). SUPERSEDED: [[kw-spectra]] "destroy it and CLOSE the combat chain; the attack does not resolve" → the trigger is a respondable layer; on resolution the aura is destroyed and the chain closes only if NO other legal attack-targets remain (8.3.14b; changed with [[release-notes-high-seas]]). SUPERSEDED: [[kw-phantasm]] "destroy the attack and close the chain" → trigger re-checks its condition at resolution (fails if {p} drops below 6, 5.3.2a); the chain closes ONLY if the attack is destroyed before its link's damage calculation — after damage, the chain stays open (8.3.13b). SUPERSEDED: "attacking an ally means there is no defending hero" → since [[release-notes-compendium-of-rathe]], the ally's controller IS the defending hero. New rule that debuted with Monarch and still stands: both players have priority during the Defend Step. Page errata (effective 2021-05-04 CR): Seeds of Agony rewritten (next cost-N-or-less attack action gains the on-attack ping; stacked instances = separate sources, AB1 needed per instance); Vestige of Sol rewritten as a replacement effect. Still-good rulings: Levia uses last-known info for 6+{p} banishes; soul-hits (Wartune Herald) do NOT close the chain — link and defenders remain, prior go again pays via LKI; Spectral Shield is NOT optional; Exude Confidence's instant works in the defend step before DRs; Bolting Blade's cost stays 4 when played for 0; alternative costs (Rise Above) skip the cost but still pay increases; Merciful Retribution: graveyard-from-chain and discard are NOT "destroyed"; no priority mid-resolution.
