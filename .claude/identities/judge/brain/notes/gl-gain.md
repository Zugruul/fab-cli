**Gain** (CR Glossary): general effect: An effect keyword that universally means that the properties of an object change by gaining a property, gaining part of a property, and/or increasing the value of a numeric property. Gains is synonymous with "gets", "has", and "is"; and is the antonym of "loses".[8.5.8][8.5.9] life effect: An effect keyword that means to increase the life total of the hero or another living object.[8.5.7] resource effect: An effect keyword that means to increase the resource assets of a player.[8.5.7] Index: [[glossary-index]].
