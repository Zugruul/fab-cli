ACCESSIBILITY (TRP 3.10): TO must make reasonable efforts; case-by-case rule exceptions allowed if they give NO strategic advantage, all pre-approved by the Head Judge. Examples: caretaker for shuffling/drafting with physical disability, fixed play-table, pre-written communication phrases, translation devices during judge calls. Note: accessibility assistance is exempt from outside-assistance rules (TRP 5.9). COVERAGE (3.11): participation = consent to publication of name/decklist/results; coverage may direct logistics in feature matches (extra markers, noise-cancelling headphones, IP-clean materials); Head Judge MAY use official video replays for rulings during a match and for post-match investigations; declining a feature: penalty-free in Swiss at casual/competitive REL, but MATCH LOSS at professional REL or in play-offs at any REL; spectators may record unobtrusively — obtrusive recording needs TO + both players' permission.
