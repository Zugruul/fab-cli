Outsiders (legal 2023-03-24, 239 cards) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: [[kw-stealth]] — an EMPTY keyword with no inherent rules meaning, only referenced by other effects (Uzuri, Prowl, Spikes). [[kw-quiver]] — equips in a weapon slot IN ADDITION to a 2H bow (equip-rule exception); max 1; can exist without a weapon. Uzuri, Switchblade: any card may pay the banish (only the SWAP-IN must be a ≤2-cost attack action); target stealth attack must be on the ACTIVE link; the replacement attack is NOT "played" — no targets/modes/on-play/on-attack triggers; defenders and effects ON defenders stay, but layers targeting the removed card fizzle; swapped-in attacks still see "attack reaction played this link" conditions (Shake Down on-hit, Sneak Attack +4). [[kw-trap]]s: event-based "when this defends" — condition checked at the moment the trap resolves/defends, later fulfillment = no trigger; NO trigger vs ally attacks; Riptide pings whenever a trap TRIGGERS even if its effect does nothing. Buzzsaw Trap strips all power gains retro- and proactively (back to base). Tarpit Trap prevents ALL effects on the next attack-action hit (even a later chain); go again still grants the action point (resolution ability, not trigger); prevented "next/first time" triggers are consumed. Dagger flicking (Flick Knives/Hurl): dagger hit = hit event, CHAIN LINK has hit (Mask of Momentum yes) but the ATTACK has not (on-attack-hit no); dagger destroyed regardless. Malign/Murkmire Grapnel "damage can't be prevented": replacements still do their non-prevention parts (Ward still self-destroys). Mask of Many Faces/Be Like Water: gained name is ADDITIONAL, lost on zone change; only real format-legal card names; tokens can't be named. Amnesia: owned cards/tokens lose names; self-references read as "this"; nameless = immune to name-effects. Infiltrate: you control but opponent OWNS the played card (clears to owner's graveyard). "Can't be played from hand" beats permissions (can't > can). ERRATA on this page (2023-03-15): Back Heel Kick and Give and Take pages show corrected intended text; bottom-of-deck effects errata'd to "bottom of the OWNER's deck".
