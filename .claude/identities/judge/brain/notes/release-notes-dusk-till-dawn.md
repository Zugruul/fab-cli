Dusk till Dawn (legal 2023-07-14, 236 cards) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: [[kw-awaken]] ("flip it") — back face becomes the ONLY active face; can't awaken single-sided or already-flipped cards. [[kw-rune-gate]]: with Runechants ≥ the card's {r} cost, play it from banished (face-up only) without paying {r} — cost set to 0 THEN modifiers apply (Frostbite still charges). [[kw-mirage]]: "defending a non-Illusionist 6+{p} attack → destroy this" — condition must hold BOTH at trigger AND at resolution. [[kw-solflare]]: triggers ONLY on charging to your OWN hero's soul (other soul-puts don't count). [[kw-unity]]: triggers when this becomes defending SIMULTANEOUSLY with a card from hand. [[kw-figment]]: DFC enters arena figment-side; resets to figment on leaving. [[kw-demi-hero]]: starts in inventory, can't be starting hero; becoming one RESETS life to its value (not gain/loss — Poison the Well inert); hero-specific effects don't carry; still a hero. Figment Archangel timing: destroyed before attack resolution → chain closes; after → it doesn't; destroyed while its on-attack trigger is stacked → chain closes but trigger still resolves; with go again destroyed during resolution step → LKI still grants the action point. Blasmophet, Levia Consumed: transforms only at EXACTLY 13 life from [[kw-blood-debt]]; blood-debt triggers resolve one at a time (transform between them switches loss→banish); old hero becomes the demi-hero's soul. Face-down banished cards: ALL abilities off, incl. play-from-banish. Oblivion needs exactly 6 Runechants at declaration. Morlock Hill must resolve before lethal; prevention set to exact incoming damage; goes inactive if damage becomes non-lethal. Cleansing Light can destroy an aura ON THE STACK — its layer fails. Numbskull locks cost/{p}/{d} in ANY zone. Reality Refractor: attacking with an aura doesn't inherit its go again. Chains of Mephetis banish-instead-of-draw ≠ drawing. Courage/Eloquence: destroying the token before its trigger resolves does NOT stop the buff. "Lost life this turn" counts payments, effects, and damage; regaining doesn't erase it.
