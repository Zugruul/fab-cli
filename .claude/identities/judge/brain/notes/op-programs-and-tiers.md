Organized Play programs by tier and REL (TRP A.1): Tier 4 — World Championship, Pro Tour (both Professional REL). Tier 3 — National Championship USA (Professional), other Nationals (Competitive), Calling day 2+ (Professional), Calling day 1 (Competitive). Tier 2 — Sunday Showdown, Battlegrounds, ProQuest, Road to Nationals (all Competitive). Tier 1 — Skirmish, Armory, Pre-Release, World Premiere, On-demand (all Casual).
