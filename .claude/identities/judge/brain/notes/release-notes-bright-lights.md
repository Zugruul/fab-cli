Bright Lights + Round the Table (legal 2023-10-06, 251 cards) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: [[kw-ambush]] (defend from arsenal if otherwise legal); [[kw-crank]] (as item enters, remove steam counter for an action point — APs only gainable during YOUR action phase); [[kw-modular]] (equips to any of YOUR Arms/Chest/Head/Legs, takes the zone's subtype; never opponent zones or weapon/off-hand/quiver); [[kw-protect]] (defend another hero attacked by an opponent: defending hero declares FIRST, then protect cards add, all become defending simultaneously; can't violate defend restrictions like dominate's one-from-hand; protecting from hand doesn't enable [[kw-reprise]] — you're not the defending hero; you CONTROL the protect card while it defends); [[kw-scrap]] (optional additional cost, declared before paying anything else); [[kw-evo-upgrade]] (Teklovossen, the Mechropotent counts as 4 Evos); [[kw-galvanize]] (destroy exactly ONE item per trigger); [[kw-tower]] (bonus at 13+{p} attack); Block TYPE ([[kw-block]]): can't be played, declared as a normal defending card — NOT a defense reaction. Singularity/Mechropotent: transform requires hero+weapon+4 Evos or nothing; base life = hero's life at resolution; if it leaves the arena you LOSE the game; defending with it never triggers Phantasm (not an attack action card). Evo equipment are deck cards that are both action/instant AND equipment (overpower/piercing apply); transforming a DEFENDING base removes it from the chain link — Battleworn/Temper/Blade Break won't fire at close; bases put under Evos become NEW objects (counters lost). Twin Drive: declare boost count before costs; 2 boost triggers but max 1 AP from go again. Master Cog: counter can be added while the crank item is on the stack, but not to not-yet-created tokens (Hyper Driver). Go again must be gained before the Link step; on-hit grants before the Damage step — never retroactive. Name errata: "Qi Unbound" is to be treated as "Qi Unleashed" (Dynasty original); Hammer of Havenhold "Chivarly" → "Chivalry".
