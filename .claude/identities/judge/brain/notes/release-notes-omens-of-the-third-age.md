Omens of the Third Age (legal 2026-06-05, 251 cards + IAR) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: [[kw-fragment]] ("whenever a card with 2+{d} defends this, this gets -2{p}") — one trigger PER qualifying defender, event-based (resolves even if defender loses {d}/leaves), still "fragments" at {p}≤0, no retro trigger if gained mid-defense; [[kw-quickstrike]] ("if this has go again, [effect]") — continuous, not functional while defending, gaining go again late grants the bonus but granted "when this attacks" abilities do NOT retro-trigger; [[kw-starfall]] ("if an instant was put into your graveyard this turn") — checked at resolution, targets still declared at play, the card's own graveyard arrival can't enable itself. Holo counters (Lightning Illusionist): banish a Lightning aura with no holo counters, return +1 counter; tokens banished cease to exist; "Lightning aura" = Lightning supertype + Aura subtype (Lightning Flow is Elemental, NOT Lightning). Attack queue (Beckon Steel): attacks generated mid-chain-link wait in a stack sub-area, become new chain links at the Resolution Step (no layer step); chain can't close while an attack is queued; "when this attacks"/"next attack" effects apply only on entering the chain; queued attacks are targetable. Ward-aura timing theme: destroying a [[kw-ward]] aura stacks its leave-arena trigger AFTER damage finalizes — its output never applies to the same damage event (cf. Starflow Robes too late vs Glide Through Starlight sub-event, eligible). Other: Aurora's Embodiment must exist BEFORE the attack is played; duality-cycle hand instants = activated abilities, not "playing an instant" (no Starfall feed); Step Between restriction beats permissions; Stormshard alt cost 0 still takes increases (Frostbite); "Runechant of X" cards are Runechants only in arena; Fractal Creation token copy DOES trigger enter-arena abilities, no counters copied.
