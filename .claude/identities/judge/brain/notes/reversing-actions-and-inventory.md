REVERSING ACTIONS (TRP 5.4): committed actions (verbal or non-verbal) are final — no takesies-backsies, and coercing an opponent into allowing a reversal is prohibited. A Judge MAY allow a change only when certain the player gained NO information since the action (immediate "sorry, meant to pitch this other card" / instant "wait, I'll block" — allowed; any doubt about information gained → refuse). Dexterity errors are not actions. Distinguish from rules-mandated reversals of ILLEGAL actions (CR 1.10.3, [[game-state-actions-and-reversal]]) and from infraction fixes ([[fixing-game-states]]). INVENTORY (4.10): the unselected remainder of the registered card-pool; private — players may look at their own, never the opponent's; must be kept completely separate from cards in the game and from other card-like objects.
