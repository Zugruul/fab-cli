Double-faced/split rulings (CR 9): naming — one face/side only, never both (9.1.2b, 9.2.2b); "same name as a split-card" requires ALL its names (Bonds of Agony + Null||Shock, 9.2.2c). Effects tracking a double-faced card survive face changes while the object persists (Oasis Respite on a flipping construct, 9.1.2c). Face-activity table: flip = front outside game/on reset, back on arena entry (construct/invocation); twin = both active outside arena/stack, one in play chosen by the player/effect (9.1.4b); transcend = back stays active permanently even through object resets — the 3.0.9 exception (9.1.5b). Split on stack: chosen side only, other side's name/typebox/textbox DON'T EXIST thereafter (9.2.3); deckbuilding needs both sides' supertypes subset (9.2.2a). Marked removal happens inside the hit event — not a state action, not a trigger, so nothing can respond between hit and unmark (9.3.3). Living Legend: status defined solely by the live page (9.4.2) — see [[card-legality-live-fetch]]. Links: [[keywords-index]], [[zone-move-and-reset-rulings]].
