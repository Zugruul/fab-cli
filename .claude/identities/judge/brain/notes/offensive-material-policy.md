Infringing & offensive material (TRP 4.12): nobody may possess LSS-IP-infringing material (unofficial playmats/merch with LSS art or logos, counterfeit cards) or non-LSS material that is offensive/disruptive (overly sexualized sleeves/mats; racist, homophobic, transphobic, sexist, ableist, hateful material). TO requests removal; refusal → DQ (players) or venue ejection (non-players). TO determines acceptable non-LSS material; IP policy detail lives in the LSS Terms of Use for Game and Studio Assets.
