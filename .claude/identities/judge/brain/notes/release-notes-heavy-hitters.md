Heavy Hitters (legal 2024-02-02, 255 cards) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: [[kw-beat-chest]] (optional additional cost: discard a 6+{p} card, discarder chooses; only paying THE cost counts as "beaten chest"); [[kw-clash]] (both reveal top card, greatest power wins; power modifiers apply to revealed cards; tie/no-power/no-card = nobody wins); [[kw-guardwell]] (at chain close, if it defended, -1{d} counters equal to its {d}; 0{d} = none; resolves BEFORE "this combat chain" effects end); [[kw-universal]] (in any zone shares controller's hero CLASS, not talent); [[kw-wager]] (delayed trigger at chain-link resolution: hit → you create the token, else opponent does; last-known info if attack left; chain closed early (Phantasm) = nobody; hit-effect prevention does NOT stop wagers; multi-target = independent wagers per hero). Kayo, Armed and Dangerous: exactly 1 weapon zone from reveal (no 2H, no off-hand); his +1{p} to owned attack actions applies in every zone EXCEPT the combat chain (counts for clashes/beat-chest, not while attacking/defending). Victor Goldmane: only Gold created by effects YOU control triggers him; re-clash replaces the old result; The Golden Son/Thunk/Wallop trigger only if the WINNING clash revealed that card. Aurum Aegis is "Gold" but NOT a "Gold token". No Fear: 6+{p} required BEFORE banishing. Command Respect/Concuss: power-above-base checked at trigger resolution, not on hit. Gauntlets of Iron Will: reduces only the first power-GAIN event per chain link (set/decrease/+0 unaffected). Double Down vs Ripple Away: turn player orders competing token replacements; Ripple-to-0 first kills Double Down. [[kw-temper]] destroys only at chain close at 0{d} having defended. Send Packing banish ≠ intimidate. Cintari Sellsword: "your next attack" effects apply to ally attacks but attack REACTIONS can't be played during them. Deathmatch Arena: only lethal DAMAGE (not life loss) makes Gold. Links: [[keyword-interaction-rulings]], [[release-notes-index]], [[kw-token-gold]], [[kw-token-might]], [[kw-token-vigor]].
