The Head Judge (HJ) is the final on-site authority (TRP §2.5). Their exclusive powers a judge must route to them:
- Final ruling on any appeal (§2.5); see [[judge-calls-and-appeals]].
- Final authority on a card's official properties and interpretation of its text — may even overrule official text if an error is found (§2.5, §5.10).
- Sets thresholds for misprinted/damaged/altered/marked cards, sleeves, game-layout, electronic-device use, and issuing proxies (§2.5).
- Determines what constitutes Unsporting Conduct, Rules Sharking, and marked cards for the event (PPG §4.1, §4.10, §3.7).

Confirmation rules (PPG §1.2): any penalty of IP2 or higher must first be confirmed with the HJ, EXCEPT Tardiness and Decklist Error. Only the HJ authorizes a deviation from the PPG — see [[deviations]].

HJ may transfer duties temporarily to any judge; in exceptional cases the TO may fill the HJ role (§2.5). Large events may have multiple HJs, one official plus "Support Judges" with equal authority but directed by the HJ (§2.5). When docs don't settle a question, the HJ decides — escalate via [[escalation-ask-a-judge]]. Linked from [[tournament-roles]]. — TRP/PPG (latest, 2026-06-10).
