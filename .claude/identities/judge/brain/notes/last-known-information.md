LKI ruling tool (CR 1.2.3): destroyed/ceased objects are evaluated by their snapshot immediately before ceasing — including counters and applied effects (Auric Shards keeps its holo-counter condition, 1.2.3b). Immutable: post-hoc condition changes cannot alter the snapshot (Luminaris example, 1.2.3c). Not an object → not targetable (1.2.3d). Use LKI to resolve "the card is gone, does X still happen" disputes (Midas Touch cost lookup, Endless Arrow action point). Links: [[fixing-game-states]], [[triggered-effects-and-ordering]].
