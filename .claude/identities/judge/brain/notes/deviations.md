A deviation is a Head-Judge-authorized departure from the PPG's recommended procedure and/or penalty for an exceptional situation (PPG §1.2). Only the Head Judge may deviate, to keep deviations consistent across the tournament; any judge can and should recommend one to the HJ. When deviating, the HJ explains the standard policy AND the reason to all involved.

Reasons to deviate (§1.2, non-exhaustive):
- A technically-Warning gameplay infraction where a different procedure yields a far more equitable state.
- The standard procedure/penalty doesn't fairly address exceptional circumstances.
- Many related infractions at once where only an increased penalty equalizes the advantage gained.

Method: start from the most relevant infraction and diverge with the three principles in mind — will this help players learn (education)? make it fair (equity)? keep great games (mission)? See [[ppg-philosophy]].

Caution: ruling within the guide protects the judge (complaints target policy, not the person); deviations lack that protection and reduce the consistency players rely on, so weigh them carefully (§1.2). Repeated-infraction cap still applies: gameplay/tournament upgrades stop at Game Loss (§1.4). Escalate genuinely novel questions via [[escalation-ask-a-judge]].
