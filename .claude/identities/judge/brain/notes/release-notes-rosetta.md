Rosetta + 1st Strike (legal 2024-09-20, 258 cards) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: Split cards — one card, two names/typeboxes; deck-legal only if BOTH sides fit the card pool; announced side is the only side while played; continuous effects apply per chosen side. [[kw-meld]]: play one or both halves; melded resource cost = 2× base THEN modifiers (base 2 + Frostbite = 5); ONE layer resolving TWICE (right side first, then left) with priority between resolutions — negatable in between (first resolution's effects stand); go again on either half = whole card has it, but the action point comes only from the SECOND resolution. [[kw-macro]]: new object type — not a card/token/permanent, can't be named/chosen/targeted, ceases to exist on leaving arena, exists before heroes are presented (limited only). [[kw-arcane-shelter]] X: MANDATORY — destroy this to prevent X arcane damage; no carryover; doesn't apply if damage already 0; destroyed even if prevention is disabled (like [[kw-ward]]). [[kw-decompose]]: banish 2 Earth + 1 action card from graveyard (3 cards; an Earth action satisfies only one requirement); chosen at trigger resolution, not targeted. Earth [[kw-bond]]: the Earth card must be pitched WHILE paying THAT card's cost. Lightning Flow label: must PLAY a Lightning-typed card — activating an ability or playing only a non-Lightning half doesn't count. [[kw-amp]] clarified: replacement on the NEXT single arcane damage EVENT; multi-target attacks amp only the first event; 0-damage doesn't consume. Rulings: Null can target a melded action||instant even between resolutions; Oscilio may discard a split card with instant on EITHER side; Lightning Greaves fails on a card that already has go again; Sigils destroyed via Arcane Shelter stack their leave-arena trigger AFTER the damage finalizes; "4+ Earth banished" statics recheck constantly, face-down banished don't count, no damage recalc after the damage step; Electromagnetic Somersault — returning the attacker during/after the resolution step doesn't close the chain (LKI); turn player orders competing token-replacements (Ripple Away to 0 first → Florian never applies); Widow equipment: owner orders Arcane Barrier vs Spellvoid; beginning-of-action-phase triggers CAN be responded to, end-phase triggers can NOT.
