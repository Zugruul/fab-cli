Arcane Rising (legal 2020-03-27, 219 cards) release-notes rulings — OLD PAGE, verified against CR 2026-06. Arcane damage: can't be defended by {d} from hand or defense reactions; only [[kw-arcane-barrier]]/arcane-or-any prevention; you may prevent arcane damage you'd deal yourself. Arcane Barrier X: STILL a prevention/replacement effect with NO response window (confirmed current: 8.3.8 + 6.4.10 — prevention effects are replacement effects); per damage EVENT per source: 3 damage from one source needs full X coverage, N separate Runechants = N separate sources, one AB1 prevents each for {r} apiece; equipment doesn't move to the chain or get destroyed by use. [[kw-opt]]: top/bottom counts public, order hidden. [[kw-boost]]: chosen when played, once per instance; "considered to have boosted" regardless of reveal outcome. [[kw-reload]]: arsenal must be completely empty. [[kw-token-runechant]]: mandatory destruction trigger on playing an attack action card or weapon attack, each an independent source; Runechants created by pitching for an attack (Arknight Shard) don't trigger from THAT attack (created after it was played). Played "as an instant": type stays action; go again grants the AP only during your own action phase (1.13.2a — would-be gains outside it become zero). Forked Lightning: ONE source, two damage effects — +1 modifiers apply to both, but AB1 prevents only 1 total. Viserai: the card whose play triggered him doesn't get the +1 (hasn't resolved). Command and Conquer's DR-lock is per chain LINK. Take Cover: reload resolves pre-damage — the reloaded DR can defend the same link. Cost increases apply before reductions (Hamstring Shot). No response window between a damage event and its rider (Aether Spindle opt). SUPERSEDED: "if there are no cards left in deck, the ability does not resolve" (Ravenous Rabble) → current CR resolves partially — an effect fails only if ALL its events fail, and remaining effects still generate (1.8.9, 5.3.4b). Errata on page: Pedal to the Metal's printed dominate reminder is wrong (applies to the NEXT attack, not itself).
