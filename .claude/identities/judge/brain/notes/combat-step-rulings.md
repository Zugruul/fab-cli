Combat rulings beyond [[combat-chain-steps]]: chain links are neither objects nor zones; their properties = the active-attack's (LKI if it ceased — a banished Double Strike still leaves a Ninja chain link, 7.0.3c). Replacing the attacking card (Uzuri) = NEW attack, SAME target (7.0.4a). Attack Step abort conditions: can't chain-link / can't become attacking (Kabuto vs weapons) / no legal targets (Spectra died) → attack cleared from queue; empty queue → Close Step (7.2.2c). Defend legality: non-DR cards from hand + public equipment only, defense property required, can't re-declare, can't break restrictions like overpower mid-declaration (7.3.2b). DR resolution failure under dominate: first DR resolves, second fails (7.4.2d + 5.3.2b). Hit-event taxonomy (7.5.5): full prevention = no hit; source-swapped damage = no hit; suppressed hit-triggers still take damage (Stamp Authority). Close Step: no priority; permanents on the chain return to their zones, everything else cleared (7.7.5-6); mid-combat chain-close via effect happens as a game-state action (7.7.2d). "This chain link" fails to generate with no active link (7.0.3d). Links: [[attack-proxies-and-rulings]], [[game-state-actions-and-reversal]], [[last-known-information]].
