Welcome to Rathe (legal 2019-10-11, 226 cards) release-notes rulings — OLD PAGE, verified against CR 2026-06: superseded items marked. Founding keywords: [[kw-battleworn]] (-1{d} counter at chain close if it defended; equipment stays on chain until close so it can't defend twice per chain — still true, CR 7.7.5/7.3.2b); [[kw-blade-break]] (destroy at chain close if it defended — still true, 8.3.3); [[kw-combo]] (only the NAME of the last attack this chain matters, needn't have hit — still current templating, 8.4.1); [[kw-dominate]] (max 1 card from hand; a hand defense reaction counts toward the limit; equipment + arsenal DRs free — still true, 8.3.4a-b); [[kw-intimidate]] (banish a random hand card face-down, returns at BEGINNING of end phase, before the 4.4.3 procedure incl. draw-up; may target yourself; empty hand OK — still true, 8.5.10/4.4.2); [[kw-go-again]] (1 action point on resolution, never stacks — 8.3.5c). SUPERSEDED: [[kw-reprise]] "checked at the time you play the card, not retroactive" → now checked at RESOLUTION of the reprise ability — a defense reaction resolving into a defending card before the reprise layer resolves DOES satisfy it (CR 8.4.3a). SUPERSEDED: [[kw-crush]] "is a conditional hit effect" → now a triggered-static on a DAMAGE-DEALT event, 4+ damage, prevented damage still doesn't count (8.4.2). Terminology: "goes on the stack at chain close" survives but negation/removal language is now "clear" (3.0.12). Still-good rulings: boosted-attack cards (Bravo, Nimblism, Sharpen Steel) put go again on the ABILITY/action, not the attack; Dorinthea's first-weapon-hit trigger is mandatory; Rhinar-style "6+{p} discard" checks BASE power; Anothos/Kodachi check their condition continuously (can turn on mid-chain-link); "paying resource costs before additional costs" maps to the current asset-costs (5.1.7) vs effect-costs (5.1.8-9) split; Mask of Momentum needs the 3rd+ consecutive hit to be an attack action card; Heart of Fyendal has NO cost (≠ 0).
