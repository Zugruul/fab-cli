COUNTERS (TRP 4.6): any item if the type is clear; homogenous per type; a die may represent a count of one counter type. MARKERS (4.7): optional aids for VISIBLE/PUBLIC info only — NEVER for deck contents or historical/private info (anti-card-tracking rule); must be updated or removed when stale. DICE (4.8): equal-probability, readable; spindowns BANNED; roll from height with spin; off-table/ambiguous/unstable rolls reroll; electronic dice banned at pro REL; skippable if result is irrelevant. SLEEVES (4.9): deck-cards sleeved identically; DFCs need fully opaque sleeves; comp/pro bans highly reflective/holographic; Head Judge is final authority; judges may delay a sleeve-change to end of match. Electronic devices (4.11): allowed if visible to all + no strategic advice; private use needs judge permission; banned entirely during comp/pro drafts; physical records beat electronic in disputes (e.g. life totals).
