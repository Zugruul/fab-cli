Cost-payment rulings (CR 1.14): asset-cost payment order chi→resource→life→action (1.14.2a), each type in full before the next. Pitch legality: only while a cost needs paying, only cards granting needed asset types, one at a time, stop when paid (1.14.3b, 1.14.2d). Chi must be exhausted before resource points on {r} costs (1.14.2d). Unpayable cost → reverse the whole action (1.14.2b, via 1.10.3). Effect-costs: player orders multiple cost-effects (1.14.4a); infeasible → reverse (1.14.4b); but if a cost-effect's events get REPLACED mid-payment, the cost is still paid (1.14.4c). Off-turn action points never accrue (1.13.2b). Counters: not objects, no properties; property-counters modify as a RULE (not effect) but stage with continuous effects (1.15.2a); +1/-1 diametric counters coexist — they don't annihilate (1.15.4).
