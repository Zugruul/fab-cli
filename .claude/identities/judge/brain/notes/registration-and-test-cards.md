Decklist registration requirements (TRP A.5): Professional + Competitive REL — required for constructed AND limited; Casual — not required (unless TO specifies); Welcome Deck — never. Constructed decklist format: English name + pitch + quantity, grouped as arena-cards / pitch-1 (red or pearl) / pitch-2 (yellow) / pitch-3 (blue). Limited decklist: every non-basic card in the pool + the chosen hero. AUTHORIZED TEST PRINTS (A.6, tournament-legal): Ironrot Helm/Chest/Gauntlet/Legs [OXO001-004-P] (Slingshot Underground, white-back non-foil) and Bravo Showstopper + Great Library of Solana (High Seas Treasure Pack cold-foil full-art double-sided). Everything else non-standard = not official ([[tournament-materials-cards]]). Links: [[decklists-registration]], [[deck-checks]].
