Super Slam (legal 2025-09-26) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: [[kw-suspense]] (Guardian auras enter with 2 suspense counters via self-replacement; start of your turn remove one, NO priority; at zero a state-trigger destroys it — re-checked at resolution, so adding a counter before it resolves saves the aura); [[kw-the-crowd-cheers-the-crowd-boos]] (no inherent effect; cheers/boos don't cancel, can repeat per turn); tokens [[kw-token-confidence]] (next attack can't be defended by >2 non-block cards; multiple stack; block cards exempt) and [[kw-token-toughness]] (+1{d} to next defending action card); Revered/Reviled hero supertypes + hosers. Damage-step hard rule (recurring): an "if"-conditional turning on DURING the damage step applies the bonus but damage is NOT recalculated; go-again conditionals check at the resolution step only (no retroactive action point); late-gained "when this hits" abilities don't retro-trigger. Kayo, Underhanded Cheat fixes 1 weapon zone from reveal even if he loses abilities; Shiyana copying keeps her 2 zones; base-{p}-setting applies before other modifiers (Big Bully doubles AFTER a set-base). Lyath Goldmane halves base {p}/{d} (arena/stack, round up) before other modifiers. A Good Clean Fight: defender-OWNED non-equipment cards lose/can't gain abilities (additional costs skippable, alternative costs/Ambush unusable); stolen/opponent-owned cards you control unaffected. [[kw-clash]] package: revealing an OPPONENT's card never triggers its clash abilities (owner/controller only); The Old Switcheroo swaps only the first clash; Victor Goldmane re-clash triggers the newly revealed card normally. Charms: choose any number of modes (incl. 0), printed order, no priority between effects. Chains of Eminence: if top card can't be pitched, pitch-reveal effects fail entirely. Gallow, End of the Line: prevented on-hit triggers still consume "next/first time" effects. Destroying cards in deck/banished triggers NO destroy-watchers (nobody controls those cards).
