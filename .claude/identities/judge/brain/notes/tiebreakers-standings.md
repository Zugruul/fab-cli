Match points & tiebreakers (TRP A.4): Win/Bye = 1 point, Draw/Loss = 0. Standings tiebreak sequence: (1) Match Points → (2) higher Cumulative Match Points (CMP: round-weighted fractional score rewarding EARLY wins; all-wins = 1, no-wins = 0) → (3) lower Match Loss Percentage (MLP: losses ÷ played rounds; byes don't count as played, so with any losses a bye is WORSE than a draw; draws aren't losses) → (4) lower average Opponent MLP → (5) higher average Opponent CMP → (6) random. Practical: draws beat losses for MLP; early-round wins beat late-round wins for CMP. Links: [[round-structure-and-timing]], [[draws-and-concessions]], [[swiss-rounds-and-time-limits]].
