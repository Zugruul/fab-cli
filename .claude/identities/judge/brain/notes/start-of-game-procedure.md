The start-of-game procedure begins a match; players do NOT get priority during it (CR 4.1.1). Steps (TRP §3.3, CR 4.1):
1. Place hero face-up in the hero zone.
2. A player is selected by a random mutually-agreeable method and chooses the first-turn-player. In a later game of a match, the player who lost the previous game chooses (draw → same chooser); in game 1 of a play-off round, the higher-standing player chooses (TRP §3.3, CR 4.1.3).
3. Select arena-cards (equipment, weapons) from the card-pool.
4. Select deck-cards to form the deck.
5. Remaining legal cards become the player's inventory (TRP §4.10 ref).
6. Shuffle and present the deck to the opponent to shuffle/cut — see [[shuffling-and-marked-cards]].
7. Reveal and set up arena-cards; start-of-game triggers resolve (first-turn-player orders simultaneous ones, CR 4.1.8a).
8. Draw up to intellect; the game has begun.

Time limits: 5 minutes for game 1, 3 minutes for each later game; exceeding is Slow Play (TRP §3.3). If a player makes no first-turn choice before looking at their hand, they must take the first turn (TRP §3.3). Presenting an illegal/incorrect set here is [[gameplay-infractions-overview]] (Presenting Cards Error). — TRP/CR (latest, 2026-06-10).
