Materials rulings (TRP 4): players bring cards, decklists, sleeves, tokens for their create-effects, dice, reference cards for copy-effects, pen+paper/counters/markers (4.1). Cards stored near the card-pool COUNT as card-pool at comp/pro REL unless: undecklisted event promos, tournament-issued proxies, official placeholders, or back-face DFC representations (4.1). Card legitimacy (4.2): official = genuine LSS with official/approved back-face; artist proofs, inserts, oversized = NOT cards. Properties = latest printing + errata (4.2). Non-English/misprinted cards OK if unambiguous and no advantage; misprints with objectively wrong info require informing the opponent + correct info available. Damaged cards: accidental wear OK; intentionally cut/ripped-and-reconstructed illegal. Altered cards: must not obscure name/color bar/numeric properties, no strategic assistance; comp/pro REL requires Head Judge pre-approval.
