**Spellvoid** — ability keyword (CR 8.3.15).

Spellvoid is a static ability. Spellvoid is written as "Spellvoid N" which means "If you would be dealt arcane damage, you may destroy this to prevent N of that damage." 8.3.15a If the controlling player cannot destroy the object with Spellvoid, they cannot apply the optional prevention effect.
