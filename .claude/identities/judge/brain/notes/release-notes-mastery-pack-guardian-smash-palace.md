Mastery Pack: Guardian + Smash Palace (2025-08-08) release-notes rulings. New: [[kw-crush]] label ("when this deals 4+ damage to a hero"); granted crush (Leave a Dent) makes the card COUNT as having crush — but a weapon gaining it is still not "a card you own with a crush ability" for Valda's dominate. [[kw-token-seismic-surge]]: Valda, Seismic Impact creates one trigger PER draw event ("draw 2" = one trigger, 2 tokens); her 3+-token check evaluates when the start-of-turn trigger resolves (no priority in start/end phase). Smash Palace [[kw-clash]]/[[kw-wager]] events: clash vs a hero fails (no winner) if no attacking/defending hero exists at resolution; ties = no winner. Pit-Fighter heroes (Bolfar, Kox, Crix, Reya, Victor Goldmane) are UPF-ONLY — not Blitz/Commoner despite young stats (Bravo, Flattering Showman is a normal young hero). Event Equipment (Bloodied cycle): equip only at event resolution into an EMPTY zone, never retroactively. Damage-recalc rule repeated set-wide: conditional continuous effects toggling during/after the damage step never recalc damage. Annexation of Grandeur/Forge: gained objects keep state (counters/tapped/defending); gaining control triggers no enters/played watchers; equipping via Annexation DOES trigger "when you equip"; a stolen defending card keeps defending. Blinding of the Old Ones: ability loss hits ALL zones (hand/deck/grave/outside game); cards can still GAIN abilities. Break Stature: name-stripped aura tokens don't lock the name. Visit Anvilheim: X set at declaration; cost modifiers change {r} paid, not counters. Base of the Mountain: banished no-{d} actions defend at base 0, not "from hand" for dominate. Headbutt: only head-subtype equipment may defend. Lion's Pounce: attacks every hero simultaneously (defenders clockwise); killing all others = DRAW. Reya: protect defenders declared after the attacked player's; protect can't bypass overpower/dominate.
