Conduct specifics (TRP 6, deepens [[conduct-infractions-overview]]): CHEATING = intentionally breaking rules, IGNORING someone else breaking them, or lying to an official, for advantage (6.2 — awareness of an opponent's infraction that you exploit is itself cheating). BRIBERY (6.4): no rewards for match results, drops, or in-game decisions; "concede now, I'll concede later" IS bribery. PRIZE SPLITS legal if they don't influence the result; need not be equal; players facilitate them. Head Judge should proactively remind about bribery before the last Swiss round at comp/pro. WAGERING (6.5): all betting banned, even loser-buys-dinner. STALLING (6.6): intentionally playing to the clock for a draw, or milking judge calls for extensions. Aggressive behavior/harassment: zero tolerance; alcohol/drugs prohibited; hygiene enforceable (6.3). Buy/sell/trade: store or TO sets floor rules; only FAB singles tradeable at LSS events without permission (6.7). Links: [[cheating-vs-error]], [[slow-play-vs-stalling]], [[draws-and-concessions]].
