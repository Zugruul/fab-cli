Logistics rulings (TRP 5.9-5.14, extends [[shortcuts-and-out-of-order-play]] and [[note-taking-rules]]): OUTSIDE ASSISTANCE — no spectator advice or pre-made notes during a match/draft, BUT personal notes + devices ARE allowed during the start-of-game procedure (sideboard guides legal between games!); accessibility assistance exempt; silence during comp/pro drafts (5.9). CARD ID — partial names OK if unambiguous; official text = latest English printing + errata; players may request official text; Head Judge may overrule erroneous card text (5.10). SHUFFLING — randomize + present; opponent cuts or shuffles; pile counting alone is NOT shuffling; seeing faces mid-shuffle = reshuffle (5.11). RANDOM SELECTION — dice with pre-announced mapping, equal likelihood; or shuffle-and-opponent-picks if unmarked (5.12). MARKED CARDS — distinguishable-from-behind test; sleeved = judged in-sleeve; Head Judge decides; examples include one flat card among curled foils (5.13). DEFAULT DECISIONS (5.14): undeclared damage/attack target → opposing hero (no default with multiple opposing heroes); unacknowledged optional effect → declined (Fyendal's Spring Tunic counter waived if the player acted past it).
