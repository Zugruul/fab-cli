Tales of Aria (legal 2021-09-24, 238 cards) release-notes rulings — OLD PAGE, verified against CR 2026-06. Introduced: Elemental talent (Earth/Ice/Lightning; Elemental cards playable by any Elemental hero; Earth/Ice/Lightning cards need matching [[kw-essence]]); [[kw-fusion]] (optional additional cost: reveal a matching-element card from hand — NOT the pitched card; playable unfused; can't fuse while defending but CAN fuse a defense reaction since those are played; a card with X-fusion is not an X card — can't pay [[kw-channel]] or fuse its twin; one dual-element card satisfies a dual fusion); [[kw-channel]] (beginning of YOUR end phase: add a flow counter, destroy unless you bottom one matching TRUE-element card from pitch per counter — fusion cards don't count; bottomed order is your hidden choice); off-hand equipment type (weapon-zone slot, max one, incompatible with 2H — current framing 8.2.10 unchanged in effect); [[kw-spellvoid]] (replacement, no response window). SUPERSEDED: "playing a non-attack action closes the combat chain" (Ball Lightning ruling) → non-attack actions now CANNOT be played while the chain is open except as instants (7.0.1a); the chain closes only via the Close-Step conditions (7.7.2). CONFIRMED still true (page + current CR agree): no priority during start/end phases (4.2.1/4.4.1) — end-phase triggers like channel are not respondable; [[kw-dominate]]/[[kw-blade-break]]/[[kw-go-again]] work as currently templated. Page errata: Korshem's printed "storybook" text is replaced by official tournament text (page quotes it in full); page inconsistency: Channel Mount Heroic "age counter" should read flow counter. Still-good rulings: [[kw-token-frostbite]] — cost increase applies before reductions and after alternative-cost declaration; while its destroy-trigger is on the stack, responding plays STILL pay the extra {r}; doesn't tax triggered/static abilities. Oldhim must pitch Earth/Ice as THIS ability's cost (prior pitches don't count; a dual Earth+Ice card gives both). Frost Lock: X-cost cards are cost 0 off-chain → unplayable/unpitchable under it; no-cost cards (Heart of Fyendal) unaffected. Briar: per-instance damage triggers. Mark of Lightning/Buzz Bolt damage is NOT arcane — only generic prevention works. Heart of Ice taxes: defending is free, but defense reactions and weapon attacks (activated abilities) pay. Korshem triggers once per reveal EVENT; fusing an attack doesn't give that attack the +1{p} mode. Links: [[keyword-interaction-rulings]], [[release-notes-index]], [[kw-flow]], [[kw-arcane-barrier]].
