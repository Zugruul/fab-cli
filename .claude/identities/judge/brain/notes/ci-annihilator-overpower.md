**Annihilator Engine** (Mechanologist Action Attack) scales with equipped Evos: "...- 3 or more, this gets overpower, - 4 or more, this gets +3{p}." Overpower (CR 8.3.22) means "This can't be defended by more than one action card."

Ruling: like dominate, overpower only gates future ADD-DEFEND events (CR 8.3.22a). If the Evo count is below 3 at the moment a defender adds a second action card (legal at the time), and a later effect brings the Evo count to 3+ (granting overpower) before the chain link resolves, CR 8.3.22b applies: "If an attack is defended by two or more action cards and then the attack gains overpower, no cards are retroactively removed from defending." The already-added second action-card defender stays.

Practical judge note: since Annihilator Engine's overpower is a continuously-checked static condition (tied to current Evo count), it can also be LOST if Evos are removed after the attack starts resolving — but 8.3.22a/b only speak to defending being added, not to removing cards already legally added, so a fluctuating Evo count does not retroactively re-open or re-close prior add-defend windows either way once a card is on the chain link.
