Dynasty (legal 2022-11-11, 247 cards) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: [[kw-construct]] (DFC resolves flipped as an arena permanent; reverts to construct face as a NEW object outside stack/arena); [[kw-ephemeral]] (would go to a graveyard from ANYWHERE → ceases to exist instead); [[kw-overpower]] (defender can't defend with more than one action card — does NOT stop extra action cards added by EFFECTS, e.g. Amulet of Havencall); [[kw-piercing]] X (+X{p} if defended by an equipment; not per-equipment, but multiple piercing instances stack); [[kw-contract]] (completable any number of times while face-up in arena/attacking, not defending; YOU must do the instructed banish; simultaneous banishes complete once per card); [[kw-material]] (card under a permanent granting an ability; untargetable/uncontrolled underneath); [[kw-surge]] ("deals MORE than N" checked AFTER prevention; no priority window between damage and surge). Emperor, Dracai of Aesir: activating him IS an attack (opens chain, needs target); attacking with the searched Command and Conquer is NOT "playing" it ("next attack action you play" effects no; "your next attack" effects yes). Rok "can't be prevented": replacements still do everything except prevent (Ward equipment still destroyed). Nitro Mechanoid must transform ALL targets or self-negates; can't attack with nothing underneath. Crouching Tiger: a real card, NOT a token — no pitch/defense property; copies don't count toward format limits. Additional-cost discards resolve AFTER the card is played (can't fund it); choosing to discard via a random method ≠ "random discard". Start-of-game equip triggers (Seasoned Saviour, Crown of Dominion) resolve before first hands, mandatory. Yoji, Royal Protector: replacement for hits/arcane damage, NOT life loss, can't target self. Spirit of Eirina: arena-instead-of-soul still counts as "Charged" but NOT "entered your soul". Looming Doom: no end-phase priority — only already-active prevention works. Scramble Pulse: -1{d} on all defending equipment; [[kw-temper]] at 0{d} destroyed at chain close. 0{d} equipment CAN defend (has the property); no-defense equipment can't. Immobilizing Shot: 1 attack + 1 non-attack action next phase regardless of APs; as-instant plays still count as actions. Spectral Prowler: Shield condition checked at trigger RESOLUTION (created in between still counts). Runeblade dual-pitch cycle: only pitches that are part of an actual cost count — no over-pitching.
