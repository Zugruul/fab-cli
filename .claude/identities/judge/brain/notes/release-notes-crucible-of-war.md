Crucible of War (legal 2020-08-28, 198 cards) release-notes rulings — OLD PAGE, verified against CR 2026-06. New then: [[kw-negate]] — SUPERSEDED terminology: negated layers are now CLEARED from the stack (8.5.26); clear = owner's graveyard for cards, tokens cease to exist (3.0.12/3.0.12a); clearing is NOT destruction (8.5.4a). [[kw-temper]]: -1{d} counter when chain closes if it defended, destroy at 0{d}; counters from OTHER sources never destroy it even at 0 (still true). Errata on page: Ira, Crimson Haze gains subtype Young; Katsu, the Wanderer text update is non-functional. SUPERSEDED: [[kw-reprise]] "checked when played, not retroactive" → checked at RESOLUTION (8.4.3a). SUPERSEDED: [[kw-crush]] "is a conditional hit effect" → damage-dealt triggered-static (8.4.2). STILL TRUE per current CR: [[kw-intimidate]] returns at beginning of end phase before draw-up (8.5.10/4.4.2); [[kw-dominate]] hand-DR counts toward the 1-hand-card limit (8.3.4b); Shiyana MAY play any-hero specialization cards — [[kw-specialization]] is a meta-static restricting DECK INCLUSION only, and Shiyana's text permits them in deck (8.3.7). Rulings that hold: Kano's ability is an instant usable on the opponent's turn, but go again yields NO action point outside your own action phase (1.13.2a); Dorinthea/Twinning Blade extra weapon attack bypasses once-per-turn but still needs an action point; Zephyr Needle's destroy trigger snapshots when a card defends — later power pumps don't save it, but a reaction played in RESPONSE to the trigger can; Benji: power pushed above 2 lets hand cards defend but only as defense reactions (defend step passed); Mangle can't hit battleworn equipment mid-chain (no counter until close); Tripwire Trap unpaid prevents ALL hit triggers incl. hero/equipment; Shiyana copies don't reset damage taken; Fyendal's Spring Tunic can't be activated during the start phase (no priority, 4.2.1); Dash's chosen item counts toward minimum deck size.
