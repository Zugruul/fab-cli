**Draw** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.6).

Draw is a discrete effect. To draw a card, move the top card of the deck to the player's hand. 8.5.6a Putting an card into the hand because of a rule or effect other than draw is not considered drawing that card. 8.5.6b If a player tries to draw a card when there are no cards in their deck, the draw effect fails. 8.5.6c If a card is drawn but a replacement effect modifies the destination to a zone other than the hand, the card is still considered drawn.
