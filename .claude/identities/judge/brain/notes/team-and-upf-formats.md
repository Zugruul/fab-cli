TEAM format (TRP 9.2): 3 members at positions A/B/C, locked at registration (registering a non-playing member = Fraud); free intra-team communication during the match but the seated player has final say and moves pieces; team-talk is NOT outside assistance, prohibited notes still are; leaving your seat without a judge = spectator silence for the rest of the match; first team to 2 game wins; time-out → most games won, else draw; 1 missing member = forfeited game, 2+ = forfeited match. Team constructed: no duplicate HEROES on a team (same moniker different versions OK — both Bravos allowed, 9.2.1); mixed formats possible. Team sealed: 8 packs/team, cards registered per player, no post-registration exchanges. Team draft: pod = the two opposing teams, seated A1,B2,C1,A2,B1,C2; zero communication even intra-team (9.2.3). UPF (9.3): young hero, pool ≤52, deck exactly 40, ≤2 copies, typically 4 players; targeting restricted to IMMEDIATE left/right neighbors except effects saying "any hero/opponent" ("any target" does NOT bypass the neighbor rule!). IRA LEARN-TO-PLAY (9.1): fixed 30-card Ira deck list (matches the learn-to-play video deck). Links: [[limited-format-procedures]], [[event-tiers-and-rels]], [[official-formats]].
