Everfest (legal 2022-02-04, 198 cards) release-notes rulings — OLD PAGE, verified against CR 2026-06. Only new keyword: [[kw-heave]] X — hidden triggered ability at beginning of YOUR end phase: if in hand and arsenal empty, may pay X, put it face-up into arsenal, create X [[kw-token-seismic-surge]]; hand-only (never from arsenal); both parts paid in full or not at all; card must be shown when the trigger resolves; no obligation to play it later. New token: Silver. SUPERSEDED reminder text quoted on page: [[kw-spectra]] "destroy it and close the combat chain" → chain no longer closes unless no legal attack-targets remain (8.3.14b, changed with [[release-notes-high-seas]]); [[kw-phantasm]] "destroy this and close the combat chain" → closes only if destroyed before the link's damage calculation (8.3.13b). CONFIRMED still true: no priority window during the end phase (4.4.1 — heave, intimidate-return, Revel in Runeblood all resolve without responses); tokens cease to exist on leaving the arena (3.0.12a); an attack action card put into the graveyard from the chain is NOT "destroyed" (8.5.4a); go again never stacks — one AP (8.3.5c); [[kw-intimidate]] return timing beginning-of-end-phase before draw-up (8.5.10/4.4.2). Still-good rulings: Skull Crushers — rerolled dice use final value only; "Brute attacks" spans attack actions, weapons, AND ally activated attacks; destroying it keeps already-granted +1{p}. Ready to Roll is a replacement (two copies = +2 dice, ignore the 2 lowest). Nerves of Steel checks {p} at chain CLOSE — post-defend pumps flip battleworn/temper on or off. Steadfast: source chosen at resolution, applies across that source's damage events until used up; never stops loss of life. Fractal Replication copies abilities of ALL Illusionist attack actions on the chain (even opponent's defending ones — inert); "when you play" copies never fire, "when you attack" ones do; no LKI for departed cards. Iyslander: a Frostbite created in RESPONSE to a played card doesn't tax it. Aether Wildfire's +X applies to ALL players' arcane action damage. Boost condition = the ACT of boosting, reveal success irrelevant. Talisman of Tithes: draw reduced to 0 = no draw event, no draw triggers. Page typos: Miraging Metamorph/Haze Bending reminder texts name the wrong cards. Links: [[keyword-interaction-rulings]], [[release-notes-index]], [[kw-boost]], [[kw-token-silver]].
