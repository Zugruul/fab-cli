**Ash** (Token Ash): "Material - While Ash is under a permanent, that permanent has phantasm." Material (CR 8.4.5) explicitly functions only "while this is under a permanent." CR 3.0.14a: "A card is only considered to be under a permanent if specified by a rule or effect." By contrast, **Battlefield Breaker**'s Blood Debt (CR 8.3.11): "While this is in your banished zone, at the beginning of your end phase, lose 1{h}" — an ability explicitly scoped to function FROM the banished zone, no "under" requirement.

Ruling: if Ash ends up loose in the banished/graveyard zone (not attached under any permanent — e.g. cleared alongside its former host per 3.0.14e, or otherwise moved there directly), its Material ability is completely dormant: Material's while-condition ("under a permanent") is false for a free-floating card in a non-arena zone, since being merely present in banished/graveyard does not satisfy "under a permanent" absent a rule or effect saying so (3.0.14a). This contrasts with Blood Debt-type abilities, which are specifically written to be live from the banished zone itself. Judges should not assume ALL "while X" static abilities function in every zone a card can occupy — check the label's specific zone/location requirement (Material = under a permanent; Blood Debt = in banished zone) rather than generalizing.
