Zone-move rulings (CR 3.0.7-3.0.14): properties evaluated at ORIGIN as the object leaves (Levia example); private-at-both-ends = no properties at all (3.0.7a). Visibility re-evaluates BEFORE further replacements apply (Drone of Brutality: hand→public→redirected to deck→private, 3.0.8a). Reset rule: leaving arena/stack for non-arena zones = new object; buffs gone, but zone-move triggers and the moving effect keep the reference while public (3.0.9a-b), and reset-history is preserved (3.0.9c). Impossible destination: rule-moves clear the object; effect-moves simply FAIL (3.0.11 — Uzuri bottom-decking a token: token ceases instead). Tokens/macros/non-card layers leaving their zone cease to exist (3.0.12a). Sub-cards (hero's soul): not in arena, reset on entry, share top-card visibility, cleared if top-card ceases (3.0.14).
