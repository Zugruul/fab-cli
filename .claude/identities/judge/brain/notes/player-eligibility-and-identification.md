Eligibility (TRP §1.4): everyone may play except — suspended individuals (in Tier 2+ or Competitive/Professional REL), those prohibited by LSS or asked to leave by the TO, players 12-or-under without guardian permission, those barred by local law/venue, and tournament officials at Skirmish or Tier 2+ events.
- Suspended players MAY still play Casual-REL Tier 1 tournaments (with TO approval) and may spectate any event, but can't be officials.
- Banned individuals may not participate, attend, spectate, or officiate ANY FAB event and should be asked to leave (§1.4).
- LSS employees/contractors can't play Professional tournaments without written dispensation and must disclose their position to the TO (§1.4).

Identification (§1.5): every participant needs a single GEM profile with a unique Player ID; duplicate profiles must be resolved via op@fabtcg.com. Invite-only or age-restricted events (Nationals, Pro Tours, Worlds) require personal ID — proof of GEM control, birth certificate (under 13), driver licence, passport, or government/school ID.

Impersonating another player or misrepresenting identity is Fraud (PPG §4.11), see [[conduct-infractions-overview]]. Ties to [[event-tiers-and-rels]]. — TRP (latest, 2026-06-10).
