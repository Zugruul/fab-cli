LIVING LEGEND format — CC rules but LL heroes/weapons legal, own ban/restricted list (7.2). BLITZ — 1 young hero + max 52; EXACTLY 40 deck; ≤1 copy (singleton) (7.3). SILVER AGE — 1 young hero + max 55; EXACTLY 40 deck; ≤2 copies; hero AND all cards must be common/rare-rarity (ever printed at basic/common = common; ever rare and not common = rare) (7.4). Restricted card = max 1 copy; banned = 0 (7.0). All subject to meta-statics (Legendary = 1, Unlimited = any).
