Uzuri, Switchblade (Once per Turn Attack Reaction: banish a hand card face-down; if it's a ≤2-cost attack action, bottom-deck target attacking STEALTH card from the active link and put the banished card onto the link as the attacking card) × Command and Conquer (Generic attack 6{p}, cost 2: "Defense reaction cards can't be played this chain link. When this hits a hero, destroy all cards in their arsenal."). SEQUENCE: Uzuri attacks with a stealth attack → in the REACTION Step (7.4) she activates the swap, C&C (cost 2, qualifies) becomes the attacking card on the SAME link with the SAME target. DR LOCK: C&C's static applies the moment it's the attacking card — opponent can play NO defense reaction CARDS for the rest of that chain link (lock is per LINK, [[release-notes-arcane-rising]]). ALREADY-COMMITTED DEFENSE SURVIVES: defending cards and DRs that already resolved stay on the link ([[release-notes-outsiders]] — only layers TARGETING the removed stealth card fizzle); a DR still on the stack when C&C swaps in ALSO resolves (the static stops playing new ones, it doesn't counter pending layers). NOT "PLAYED": the swapped-in C&C gets no on-play triggers/targets/modes (7.0.4a) but its static + on-hit work normally, and it still "sees" that an attack reaction was played this link. ACTIVATED defense-reaction ABILITIES (equipment) are not "playing a defense reaction card" — still legal (8.1.3). Defend-Step blocking (non-DR hand cards, equipment) untouched. Practical shape: defender must commit DRs before knowing whether Uzuri swaps, or risk full DR lockout into a 6{p} arsenal-nuke.
