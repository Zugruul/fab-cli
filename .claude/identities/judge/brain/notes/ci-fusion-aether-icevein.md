**Aether Icevein** (Elemental Wizard Action): "Ice Fusion — Deal 5 arcane damage to any target. If Aether Icevein was fused and deals damage to a hero, they discard a card unless they pay {r}{r}." Fusion (CR 8.3.17): "Fusion is an optional additional-cost play-static ability... 'As an additional cost to play this, you may reveal (a/an) [SUPERTYPES] card(s) from your hand.'"

Ruling: the fusion cost is to REVEAL an Ice card from hand — not to pitch, discard, or otherwise remove it (contrast with costs like "banish a card from hand"). CR 8.3.17b confirms a player simply cannot fuse if they can't pay the cost of revealing the specified card(s) from hand (e.g. hand has zero Ice cards). Because revealing leaves the card in hand, the same Ice card can still be played, pitched, or used to fuse ANOTHER Fusion card later in the same turn — fusing does not consume or tap the revealed card in any way. CR 8.3.17c also caps how many cards may be revealed for a single fusion cost (one per listed supertype, and a single card may cover multiple supertypes at once if it matches more than one).
