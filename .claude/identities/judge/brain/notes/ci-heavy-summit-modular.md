**Summit, the Unforgiving** (Earth Ice Guardian Weapon Polearm 1H): "...Heavy - If this is the only card equipped to your weapon zones, it gets +2{p}." Heavy (CR 8.4.17): "Heavy - If this is the only card equipped to your weapon zones, [EFFECTS]." Modular equipment like **Adaptive Alpha Mold** (Mechanologist Equipment Base, Modular: "Action - 0: Equip this to another equipment zone") can be equipped to Arms/Chest/Head/Legs zones (CR 8.3.30a lists the equipment zones), not weapon zones.

Ruling: Heavy's condition is scoped specifically to "your weapon zones" (1H/2H/Off-Hand/Quiver-type slots), not all equipment zones generally. Equipping a Modular piece like Adaptive Alpha Mold to a Chest or Arms zone does NOT break Summit's Heavy bonus, since Modular equipment occupies a non-weapon equipment zone and is irrelevant to the "only card equipped to your weapon zones" check. The bonus IS broken the moment you equip ANY second weapon-zone card (even a small off-hand dagger), regardless of how minor — Heavy has no size/power threshold, just an exclusivity check on weapon zones specifically.

See [[kw-heavy]].
