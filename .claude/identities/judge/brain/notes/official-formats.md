FAB formats fall in three categories: constructed, limited, special (TRP §1.3). Officially supported: Classic Constructed, Living Legend, Blitz, Silver Age (constructed); Booster Draft, Sealed Deck, Blitz Preconstructed (limited); Ira Learn-to-Play, Team, Ultimate Pit Fight (special).

Constructed deck-building rules (TRP §7), subject to meta-static abilities like Legendary:
- Classic Constructed (§7.1): 1 adult hero + max 80 arena/deck cards; up to 3 copies each; min 60-card starting deck. Living-legend heroes/signature weapons, young heroes, and pit-fighter heroes are NOT legal.
- Living Legend (§7.2): same as CC but living-legend heroes and their signature weapons ARE legal; separate banned/restricted list.
- Blitz (§7.3): 1 young hero + max 52 cards; up to 1 copy each; exactly 40-card starting deck.
- Silver Age (§7.4): 1 young hero + max 55 cards; hero and all cards must be common- or rare-rarity; up to 2 copies each; exactly 40-card deck.

Restricted = max 1 copy; banned = not allowed (§7). What is banned/restricted/living-legend is NOT written here — it changes constantly; fetch live per [[card-legality-live-fetch]]. Deck legality errors are handled as [[tournament-infractions-overview]] (Decklist / Card-Pool / Presenting Cards). — TRP (latest, 2026-06-10).
