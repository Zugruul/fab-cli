**Pick Up the Point** (Assassin Ninja Action Attack): "When this attacks, you may retrieve a dagger from your graveyard." Retrieve (CR 8.5.51): "To retrieve a card, the player may pay {r} to equip the card." CR 8.5.51a: "The card must exist and be able to be equipped for the player to pay {r}. If the player cannot equip the card, they cannot choose to pay {r}."

Ruling: retrieve is gated ENTIRELY on legality of equipping, checked BEFORE any resource is spent — if the player's relevant weapon zone (e.g. their 1H dagger zone) is already occupied by another equipped weapon and no legal zone is open for the retrieved dagger (CR 8.5.41c: "an object can only be equipped to a zone if the zone is empty..."), the player is never even offered the option to pay {r}; the retrieve effect fails at the "may" stage with zero resources spent. There is no scenario where a player pays {r} and then the equip separately fails — the two are bound as a single all-or-nothing gate.

See [[kw-retrieve]].
