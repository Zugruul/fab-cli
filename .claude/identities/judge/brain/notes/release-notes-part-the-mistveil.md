Part the Mistveil (legal 2024-05-31, 239 cards) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: Chi ("Mystic Resource – Chi"): pitching Chi cards yields {c} spendable on {c} or {r} costs; {c} is spent FIRST before {r}; unspent {c} lost at end of turn; {r}-cost modifiers do NOT touch {c} costs. CRITICAL: "if a Chi was pitched to play this" requires actively pitching a Chi-subtype card for THAT card — paying from existing {c} points does NOT count (Gorgon's Gaze, Orihon of Mystic Tenets, etc.; Orihon's draw 3 is mandatory if met). [[kw-transcend]]: card goes to owner's hand flipped to its Inner Chi face; can transcend multiple times per game/turn. [[kw-cloaked]]: equipment equipped face-down, abilities (incl. [[kw-ward]]) non-functional; judges may demand reveal at game end for legality; turning face-up as an activation cost is impossible if already face-up; equip triggers don't fire when equipped face-down (Enigma, New Moon: flip happens on resolution, not as cost). [[kw-amp]]: replacement on ONE arcane damage event you control; 0-damage events don't consume it. "Pitched a blue this turn" conditions are historical — survive the pitch zone emptying (Potion of Deja Vu). Nuu/Gorgon's Gaze "without paying {r} cost": cost 0 then increases apply (Frostbite charges; X becomes 0 → Frost Lock blocks). Statics keep applying after a card becomes an attack, but power gained after the damage step deals nothing and go again gained after the link step gives no AP. Ward X recalculates continuously; Ward 0 still destroys preventing nothing; multiple ward instances → only one per damage event; ward-aura leave-arena value (Spectral Shield etc.) can never apply to the damage that destroyed it. Siren's Call/Intimate Inducement: no-{d} blue cards defend at base 0; attacking an ally means NO defending hero exists. Dense Blue Mist: prevented on-hit triggers still consume "next/first time" effects. Blanch: color loss not retroactive.
