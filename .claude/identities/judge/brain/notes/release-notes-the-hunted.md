The Hunted (legal 2025-01-31, 265 cards) release-notes rulings. New mechanics: Agents of Chaos — 6 [[kw-demi-hero]] cards for Arakni, Marionette; "become a random Agent" = d6 roll that does NOT count as a dice roll for effects; physical copies of all 6 required but NOT part of the registered card pool; hero effects (incl. Marked) and life total persist when becoming one. [[kw-mark]]ed: hero state "until a hero hits you" — removal on hit is immediate (not a trigger); marking a marked hero does nothing; persists through become/revert (Arakni, Shiyana) but NOT through transform (Mechropotent, Levia Redeemed). [[kw-retrieve]] (pay {r} to equip from graveyard): needs an empty appropriate zone or you don't pay/equip; DOES trigger "when you equip"; a re-equipped Kunai of Retribution is a NEW object (old delayed triggers miss it). [[kw-return-to-the-brood]]: revert to base hero; effects + life persist. Dagger "flicking": dagger deals 1 to a hero; a dagger hit is a hit event where the DAGGER and the CHAIN LINK have hit (Mask of Momentum counts) but the ATTACK has not (on-attack-hit effects don't); dagger destroyed regardless. Blood Runs Deep: trigger doesn't target, resolution flicks ALL daggers (mandatory), attacker orders; first dagger hit unmarks the defender; attack hit + dagger hit on one link = the link hit once. Kiss of Death: dagger but NOT a weapon — flickable (its on-hit works), not equippable, no attack ability. Draconic chain links count via Last Known Information while the chain is open; cost DEcreases apply AFTER increases; Demonstrate Devotion counts its own link. Fealty: next card you PLAY (not activate) becomes Draconic; the gained property can't be lost (even Erase Face). [[kw-trap]]s ("when this defends"): condition checked when it BECOMES defending — met later = no trigger; no attacking hero (ally attack) = no trigger. Take Up the Mantle copy replaces ALL printed properties; existing layer-continuous effects keep applying. Go again/conditionals checked at Link step, never retroactive. Provoke-style forced defends blocked by dominate/overpower leave the card in hand/arsenal. Links: [[keyword-interaction-rulings]], [[release-notes-index]], [[kw-token-fealty]].
