High Seas (legal 2025-06-06, 264 cards) release-notes rulings. EXPLICIT RULE CHANGES: (1) [[kw-spectra]] no longer explicitly closes the combat chain from its trigger — the chain closes only if NO legal attack-targets remain (matters for multi-target attacks like Barbed Barrage vs Arc Light Sentinel); (2) end-phase procedure now includes an untap step (arsenal → pitch-to-bottom → UNTAP all your tapped permanents → draw to intellect). New mechanics: [[kw-tap]]/[[kw-untap]] — permanents enter untapped; only arena permanents tap (incl. defending equipment and weapons/allies on the chain; NOT attack actions/DRs/blocks); no summoning sickness; a cost's {t} needs an untapped object, but an effect may select a tapped one and simply fail (no trigger). [[kw-give]]/[[kw-steal]] — control change only: NO enters-arena/played triggers, state (tapped/counters) preserved; giving an equipped object DOES trigger "when equipped", fails without an empty zone; steal ⊂ gain control, give ≠ steal. [[kw-companion]] (ally starting in play; off-hand companions use weapon slot), [[kw-perched]] (equips beside a 2H weapon; can't be ATTACKED but can be targeted/damaged), [[kw-unlimited]] (any deck count, Copper Cog), [[kw-watery-grave]] (ONLY arena→graveyard; card lands face-down — owner may look, invisible to graveyard effects/costs), [[kw-high-tide]] (continuous "2+ blues in pitch"; met after attacking still applies; off while defending), [[kw-go-fish]] (defender chooses+reveals; prevented reveal = nothing happens), Treasure Island [[kw-macro]] (shared, uncontrolled, untargetable; limited only). Rulings: "destroy a Gold" hero costs accept ANY Gold object (Aurum Aegis); destroy-top-of-deck triggers NO destroy-watchers (you don't control deck cards); Riches of Trōpal-Dhani Gold arrives after costs are paid; Marlynn triggers once per card drawn, resolved after the current layer; Barbed Barrage calculates damage per target, on-hits per target, dominate/overpower limit defenders per player; arrows trigger only when PUT face-up into arsenal (not turned face-up there); Blanch doesn't rewrite pitch history; no priority in start/end phases or within resolving trigger bundles.
