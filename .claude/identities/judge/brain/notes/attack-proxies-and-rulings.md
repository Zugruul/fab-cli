Attack disputes hinge on the proxy/source split (CR 1.4.3): proxies inherit properties but not activated/resolution abilities (1.4.3a); effects on the source transfer via inherited properties (1.4.3d Ironsong yes / Thrive no — Thrive modifies effects applying to ATTACKS, weapon buffs apply to the weapon); effects on the proxy never reach the source (1.4.3e Sharpen Steel stops when the weapon is no longer that attack). Attack-layers (Emperor's search-then-attack) are NOT attacks — targets declared for the layer carry to the produced attack (1.4.4a). Attack-target legality: opposing living object or made-attackable (1.4.5a); multi-target modifications need all targets separate and legal (1.4.5c).
