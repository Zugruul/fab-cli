When verifying a claim, don't stop at reading the claim's own written account (dossier/PR body prose) -- cross-check against artifacts the author didn't write to be read, like raw log/output files left over from the actual process (e.g. a game engine's gamelog.txt and matching deck file). These are much harder to fabricate consistently across multiple independent files than a prose narrative is, so when they independently corroborate the claim, it's meaningfully stronger evidence than the prose alone.
