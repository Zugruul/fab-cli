Blitz is a fast young-hero format (fabtcg.com/gameplay-formats/blitz/). Rules: 1 young hero (any "young" subtype hero is legal); 52-card card-pool including weapons/equipment; deck EXACTLY 40; SINGLETON — up to 1 copy of each unique card, including weapons/equipment; best-of-1, 35 min. As of Jan 1 2026 the page notes nearly all cards are legal (with exceptions per policy). Blitz is now a legacy/casual format and is not the primary competitively supported format — treat it as the singleton young-hero variant. "Unique" is again name AND color. Do not memorize its ban/suspend list: [[card-legality-pointer]]. Compare the 2-copy young format [[format-silver-age]] and adult [[format-classic-constructed]]. Hub: [[player-brain-map]].
