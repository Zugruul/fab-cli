A hero and its cards share supertypes: a CLASS (e.g. Ninja, Guardian, Runeblade, Wizard, Warrior, Brute, Mechanologist, Ranger, Illusionist, Assassin, Necromancer, Bard, Merchant, Pirate, Shapeshifter, Adjudicator) and zero or more TALENTS (e.g. Shadow, Light, Ice, Earth, Lightning, Draconic, Chaos, Mystic, Royal, Elemental) (types list: third_party/flesh-and-blood-cards/json/english/type.json). A card is legal in a hero's pool only if its class/talents are a SUBSET of the hero's (see each format note, e.g. [[format-classic-constructed]]); Generic cards fit any hero. Some heroes bend this via the Essence keyword (grant extra supertypes for deckbuilding) or Fusion (reveal-cost) ([[keyword-attack-labels]] neighbours). Never guess a hero's class/talent — verify against the card DB ([[card-corpus-and-search]]). Talents flavor a class's mechanics (e.g. Ice = freeze, Draconic = big attacks).
