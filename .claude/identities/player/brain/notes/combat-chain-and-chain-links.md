Combat is the game state where the combat chain is open (CR 7.0.1). The combat chain is a zone made up of chain links; it starts closed and opens when an attack is played (CR 7.0.2a). Each attack you make becomes a new chain link: the first attack is chain link 1, the second is chain link 2, and so on (learn-to-play transcript; CR 7.0.3a). A chain link comprises the active-attack, its source, and any defending cards (CR 7.0.3). Chaining multiple attacks in one turn (via go again) builds "chain link N" — many cards scale off being a later link (e.g. "if this is the 2nd or higher link to hit"), which rewards sequencing. See [[chain-link-steps]] for how each link resolves, [[keyword-attack-labels]] (combo/reprise reference the chain), and [[chain-closing]] for what happens at the end.
