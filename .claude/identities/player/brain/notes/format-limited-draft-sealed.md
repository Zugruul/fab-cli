Limited formats build decks from freshly opened product, not a collection. Booster Draft: players open and pass packs, drafting cards, then build (fabtcg.com/gameplay-formats/booster-draft/). Sealed Deck: each player opens 8 boosters of a set, picks a young hero from the set, builds a deck of EXACTLY 30 cards, ANY number of copies of a card, 35 min best-of-1 (fabtcg.com/gameplay-formats/sealed-deck/). Limited tests card evaluation and fundamentals over deckbuilding depth; the base gameplay loop ([[player-brain-map]]) is identical. Card-pool class/talent restrictions still apply relative to the chosen hero. No memorized bans needed; product legality is per event. Contrast constructed formats [[format-classic-constructed]], [[format-silver-age]]. Hub: [[player-brain-map]].
