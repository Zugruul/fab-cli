Defense declaration detail (deepens [[defending-from-hand]]): you may declare any number of non-defense-reaction cards from hand + public equipment you control (7.3.2a); cards need the defense property (no-{d} cards can't block; printed 0 CAN, 7.3.2b). Declaring is NOT playing — no cost, no stack (7.3.2). All declared cards defend TOGETHER as one multi-event; a later defense reaction defends ALONE (7.0.5e — Bastion of Unity's "defends together with a card from hand" needs the declare-step, not a DR afterwards). A card already defending can't be re-declared (7.3.2b). Multi-attack-target combat: defenders declared per target, clockwise (7.3.2f). "When this defends" triggers see buffs applied before the defend event (Surging Militia/Inertia Trap ordering, 7.0.5a). Links: [[block-vs-take-tradeoff]], [[combat-steps-precise]].
