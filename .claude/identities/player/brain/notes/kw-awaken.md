**Awaken** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.43).

Awaken is a discrete effect. To awaken a double-faced card, its back face becomes its active face.[9.1] 8.5.43a If an object is not a double-sided card, its back face is already active, or its back face can otherwise not be made active, the awaken effect fails.
