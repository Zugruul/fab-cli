**Pitch** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.44).

Pitch is a discrete effect. To pitch a card, put it into it's owner's pitch zone and the player gains assets equal to its pitch.[2.8] 8.5.44a If the effect instructs the player to pitch two or more cards, all of those cards must exist and be pitched, otherwise the pitch effect fails.
