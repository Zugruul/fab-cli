Not every "static ability tied to a zone" behaves the same way — a Material ability like **Ash**'s only turns on while it's actually attached under a permanent (CR 8.4.5/3.0.14a); if it's ever just sitting loose in your graveyard or banished zone (not under anything), it's doing nothing, unlike a Blood Debt card (e.g. **Battlefield Breaker**) which is specifically designed to function FROM the banished zone. When you're evaluating what a card in your bin/banished zone is still doing for you, check whether its ability actually says "while in [zone]" (still live) versus "while under a permanent" (dead unless physically attached) — don't assume passive value from Material-labeled cards once they're detached.
