Special rule: on the FIRST turn of the game, at end of turn ALL players draw up to their intellect, not just the turn-player (CR 4.4.3f; learn-to-play transcript). From every subsequent turn onward, only the turn-player draws at their own end of turn. Practically this means the player going second does not fall a card behind on turn one — both players enter turn two with a full hand. It also means the first-turn player should not "hold back" expecting the opponent to be card-light. Connects to [[intellect-and-hand-refill]] and [[turn-structure-phases]]. Who goes first is decided during the start-of-game procedure (CR 4.1.3).
