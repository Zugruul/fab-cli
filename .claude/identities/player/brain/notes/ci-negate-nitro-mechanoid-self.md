Don't play **Construct Nitro Mechanoid** as a "worst case, I still get go again" card — if you can't pay its transform cost (Mechanologist head/chest/arms/legs/weapon + 3 Hyper Drivers) and it negates itself, CR 8.5.26a means the whole layer fails to finish resolving, so the printed Go Again never triggers either (CR 8.3.5a requires all resolution abilities of that layer to resolve first). Only commit this card to your combat chain/action sequence when you're confident you can pay the full transform cost — otherwise it's a genuine dead card for the turn, not a discount "cantrip that whiffs gracefully."
