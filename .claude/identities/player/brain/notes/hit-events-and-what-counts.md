"Hit" is precise (CR 7.5.5): the attack hits only if the HIT-EVENT (game-generated {p} damage from power > total defense) actually deals damage. Full prevention (Feign Death) = no hit, no hit-triggers (7.5.5b). Life lost any other way ≠ a hit (7.5.5a). A CHAIN LINK "has hit" if any hit-event occurred while it was active (7.5.5d) — relevant to Whirling Mist Blossom-style "second chain link in a row to hit" conditions ([[card-corpus-and-search]]: ira-specialization). Damage math example: 6{p} into 3{d}+2{d} = 1 damage (7.5.2). Defender playing around hits: prevent down to exactly 0 to strip hit-riders; attacker: overpower the math or force unblockability. "When this hits" suppression exists (Stamp Authority, 7.5.5c). Links: [[hit-determination-and-damage]], [[prevention-and-damage-shields]], [[keyword-dominate-overpower]].
