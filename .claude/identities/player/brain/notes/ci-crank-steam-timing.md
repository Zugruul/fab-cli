**Assembly Module** hands you a real decision the turn it enters: crank immediately (CR 8.3.29) using its own entering steam counter for a free action point now, or hold the counter to keep the permanent alive into your next upkeep (where you'd otherwise need to remove a counter or lose it). Cranking on entry is correct when you need the tempo NOW (extra action point this turn) more than you need Assembly Module's recurring Hyper Driver search later — e.g., you're going for a big turn and don't need a second activation. If you want the search ability more than once, don't crank on the turn it enters; let the printed counter cover your next upkeep instead.
