The arsenal is a private zone below your hero holding at most ONE face-down deck-card (CR 3.3.1, 3.3.2). During the End Phase, if your arsenal is empty you may put one card from hand into it (CR 4.4.3b). Critical restriction from the learn-to-play transcript and rules: cards in arsenal can ONLY be played (CR 3.3.4) — you cannot pitch from arsenal and you cannot declare an arsenal card as a defending block (blocks are declared from hand/equipment, CR 7.3.2a). You CAN play a defense reaction from arsenal, because playing is allowed (CR 5.1.1a). Arsenal lets you "save" a card across the redraw instead of wasting it (see [[intellect-and-hand-refill]]), which is a key tempo/setup tool: [[arsenal-as-tempo-setup]].
