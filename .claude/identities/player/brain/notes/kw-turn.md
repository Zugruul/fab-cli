**Turn** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.24).

Turn is a discrete effect. To turn a card face-up or face-down, that card becomes public (face-up) or private (face-down) respectively.[3.0.3] 8.5.24a If the card already has the specified visibility, the turn effect fails.
