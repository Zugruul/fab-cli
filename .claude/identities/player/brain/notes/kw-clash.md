**Clash** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.45).

Clash is a discrete effect. To clash, the clashing players reveal the top card of their respective decks, and then the player that reveals the card with the greatest {p} value wins the clash. 8.5.45a The {p} values of the revealed cards are compared and the player that revealed the card with the greatest {p} value is considered to have won the clash by revealing that card. 8.5.45b If a player's revealed card does not have the power property, or they do not reveal a card, any other revealed card with the power property is considered to have greater power.[1.8.7] 8.5.45c If none of the players reveals a card with power greater than all other revealed cards, no player is considered to have won the clash. 8.5.45d If the heroes specified by the clash do not exist or there are less than two clashing heroes, the clash effect fails.
