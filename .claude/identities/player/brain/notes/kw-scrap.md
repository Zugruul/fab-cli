**Scrap** — ability keyword (CR 8.3.32).

Scrap is a play-static ability that means "As an additional cost to play this, you may banish an item or equipment from your graveyard." 8.3.32a If a player pays the additional cost to play a card with scrap, the player is considered to have scrapped, and the banished card is considered to have been scrapped. 8.3.32b A player cannot scrap if they cannot pay the additional cost of banishing an item or equipment from their graveyard.
