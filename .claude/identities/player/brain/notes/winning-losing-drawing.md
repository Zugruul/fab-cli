Game end (CR 4.5): you LOSE when your hero's life hits 0, you control no hero, an effect says so, or you concede (4.5.3); you WIN when all opponents lost or an effect says so (4.5.2). DRAWS are real: simultaneous 0 life (Forked Lightning both heroes, 4.5.4a), effect-draws, mutually-agreed intentional draw at any time (4.5.4c), STALEMATE (nobody CAN advance the game, 4.5.4d), DEADLOCK (nobody WILL act because acting loses — dual Invert Existence standoff, 4.5.4e). Extra turns happen immediately after the specified turn (4.0.3c). Player angle: in a losing race, check whether forcing simultaneity converts a loss into a draw.
