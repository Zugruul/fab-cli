Start-of-game procedure (CR 4.1): hero down → first-turn-player chosen (game 1: random-method winner CHOOSES who goes first; later games: whoever lost first in the previous game chooses — a real strategic choice, 4.1.3) → select equipment face-down (up to one per arms/chest/head/legs/weapon zones, hidden until game start, 4.1.4b) → select deck → the REST of your card-pool becomes your private inventory (4.1.6) → shuffle, opponent cuts, choices now locked (4.1.7a) → equip in clockwise order, start-of-game triggers fire → draw up to intellect. Equipment/deck selection being SIMULTANEOUS and hidden means matchup adaptation happens blind — knowing the meta field matters ([[hand-value-card-advantage]]). "During a player's turn" effects don't trigger during setup (Victor, 4.1.8b). Hero meta-statics that change setup stick even if the ability is later lost (Kayo 1 weapon zone, 4.1.2a).
