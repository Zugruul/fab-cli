Effect grammar that changes plays (CR 1.8): OPTIONAL "may" — you can only choose it if it can actually happen (can't Heave without paying AND arsenal space, 1.8.3b), but "may choose to" works regardless of outcome. CONDITIONAL: "Otherwise" = fires when the previous condition failed (1.8.4a); "unless they pay X" = they may pay; if not, effect happens (1.8.4b); tiered "2 or more…3 or more…" conditions all evaluated at once BEFORE effects (Enflame, 1.8.4d). TARGETED: "target" = declared when the card hits the stack, only public objects (1.8.5, 1.8.5a); NON-target subjects ("shuffle up to 3…") chosen only at resolution (1.8.5c) — key sequencing difference for hiding information. Effects referencing a property a card lacks: condition unmet (Harmonized Kodachi vs cost-less cards, 1.8.7) — defense restrictions like Find Center don't stop cost-less equipment.
