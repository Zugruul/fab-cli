**Golden Cog** — token keyword (CR 8.6.32).

A Golden Cog token is a token with the name "Golden Cog," the supertype Mechanologist, the subtypes cog and item, and the static abilities Crank and "This enters the arena with a steam counter. At the start of your turn, destroy this unless you remove a steam counter from it."
