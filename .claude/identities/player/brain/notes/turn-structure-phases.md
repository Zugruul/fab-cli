A turn has three phases in order: Start Phase, Action Phase, End Phase (CR 4.0.3a). Start Phase: start-of-turn triggers resolve, no priority (CR 4.2). Action Phase: you gain 1 action point and take actions until you stop or run out (CR 4.3) — this is where you play cards and attack. End Phase, in order (CR 4.4.3): reset ally life; optionally arsenal a card; put pitch-zone cards on bottom of deck; untap your permanents; lose all action/resource points; then draw up to intellect (all players on turn one). Knowing this order matters: you arsenal ([[arsenal]]) BEFORE you draw, and pitched cards ([[resource-cost-and-pitching]]) go to deck bottom before the draw.
