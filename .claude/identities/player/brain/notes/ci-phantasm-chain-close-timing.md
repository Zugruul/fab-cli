Against phantasm attacks like **Coalescence Mirage**, timing your power-boosting defense reactions matters more than raw stat math. Phantasm only destroys the attack when a non-Illusionist action-card defender's power is 6+ (CR 8.3.13). If you can push a defending card to 6+{p} BEFORE damage is calculated (Damage Step, CR 7.5.2), the whole combat chain closes immediately (CR 8.3.13b/7.7.2c) — this fizzles any remaining queued attacks in the same chain, a huge tempo swing if your opponent chained multiple attacks. If your power boost only lands AFTER damage is calculated, you still destroy Coalescence Mirage for value, but the chain does NOT close and subsequent chain links proceed as normal. When you have a choice of WHEN to pump a defender's power (e.g. an instant vs. a static "gets +N{p}" that applies earlier), front-load it before damage if your goal is to shut down the rest of their combat chain.
