Intimidate makes a targeted player banish a random card from their hand face-down; that card returns to their hand at the beginning of the end phase (CR 8.5.10). Effectively it temporarily strips a random card from the defender's hand for the current turn — reducing their available blocks ([[defending-from-hand]]) and reactions right when they need them, a tempo/disruption tool that supports an aggressive turn ([[tempo-and-go-again-chains]]). The player is "intimidated" even if they had no card to banish (CR 8.5.10a). It is random, so you cannot pick which card leaves. Often printed on attacks so the disruption lands before blocks are declared. Confirm each card's trigger/amount in [[card-corpus-and-search]]. Related disruption differs from blocking-suppression ([[keyword-dominate-overpower]]).
