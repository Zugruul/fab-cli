Running **Summit, the Unforgiving** for the Heavy bonus? You can freely equip Modular gear (like **Adaptive Alpha Mold**) to Arms/Chest/Head/Legs without losing the +2{p} — Heavy (CR 8.4.17) only checks your WEAPON zones, so non-weapon equipment slots are safe stacking room. What you must avoid is equipping literally any second weapon, including cheap off-hand daggers picked up as incidental value (e.g. from a retrieve/equip effect) — even a 0-power off-hand breaks Heavy the instant it's equipped. Plan your equipment package around this: maximize non-weapon-zone value pieces, keep the weapon zones Summit-exclusive.
