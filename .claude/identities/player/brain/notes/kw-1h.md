**(1H)** — subtype keyword (CR 8.2.1).

8.2.1a A (1H) object considered to be a one-hander. 8.2.1b A one-hander permanent must be equipped to a player's weapon zone. A one-hander cannot be equipped if the player does not have an empty weapon zone.
