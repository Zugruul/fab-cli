Key defensive keywords against non-block damage. Ward N: "If you would be dealt damage, destroy this to prevent N of that damage" (CR 8.3.20) — usually on a permanent you sacrifice to soak a hit. Arcane Barrier N: "If you would be dealt arcane damage, you may pay N resource to prevent N of it" (CR 8.3.8) — the standard defense against arcane damage, which normal blocks do NOT stop (arcane is dealt by effects, not the damage step — [[hit-determination-and-damage]]). Related: Spellvoid N and Arcane Shelter N (destroy a source to prevent arcane, CR 8.3.15/8.3.37). Knowing arcane damage ignores blocking is crucial when facing Wizards/Runeblades — you must hold resources or arcane-prevention, not just blocks ([[block-vs-take-tradeoff]]). Verify N per card: [[card-corpus-and-search]].
