FAB has no set rotation; instead adult heroes "retire" from CC via Living Legend points (fabtcg.com/living-legend/). Heroes earn LL points by winning certain tournament types (points per event type, divided per season). A weekly check occurs each Monday (US time); the threshold is 1000 points. When a hero crosses it, that hero AND its signature weapon attain Living Legend status and become ineligible for official CC play effective the Friday of that same week — moving to [[format-living-legend]] instead. This keeps CC's top of the meta rotating without banning cards outright. The current LL hero list and exact per-hero standing are LIVE data — never memorize them; fetch [[card-legality-pointer]] and the living-legend leaderboard. Compare [[format-classic-constructed]].
