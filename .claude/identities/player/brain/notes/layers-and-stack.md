The stack holds LAYERS awaiting resolution (CR 1.6): card-layers (a card you played), activated-layers (from activated abilities), triggered-layers (from "when/whenever" triggers). Key player fact: once on the stack a layer exists independently — destroying its source does NOT stop it resolving (CR 1.7.1a: killing Energy Potion after activation doesn't stop the {r}{r}). Controller of a layer = whoever put it on the stack (1.6.1b). Instants and reactions interact here — see [[attack-and-defense-reactions]], [[priority-and-passing]], [[abilities-and-functionality]].
