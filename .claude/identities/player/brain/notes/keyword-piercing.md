Piercing N is a static ability: "If this is defended by an equipment, it gets +N power" (CR 8.3.23). It punishes blocking with equipment — a common way defenders soak big attacks cheaply, since equipment blocks are free and equipment survives (subject to wear, [[keyword-equipment-wear]]). Against a piercing attack, blocking with equipment leaks extra damage, pushing the defender toward hand cards instead ([[block-vs-take-tradeoff]]). Piercing thus works alongside blocking-suppression keywords ([[keyword-dominate-overpower]]) to make an attack hard to answer efficiently and get on-hit effects through ([[hit-determination-and-damage]]).
