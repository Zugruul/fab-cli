During the Defend Step the defending hero may declare any number of non-defense-reaction cards from hand and/or equipment permanents to block (CR 7.3.2a). Blocking is FREE — no resource or action cost — and all declared cards are put onto the chain link simultaneously (learn-to-play transcript; CR 7.3.2d). A card can defend only if it has a defense value (0 counts) (CR 7.3.2b). You cannot block from arsenal ([[arsenal]]); arsenal cards can only be played. This is why leftover hand cards are never truly wasted — they become "free" defense, one of the biggest reasons to think in terms of [[hand-value-card-advantage]]. Some attacks restrict blocking: dominate limits to one card from hand, overpower to one action card ([[keyword-dominate-overpower]]). The block-or-take decision is [[block-vs-take-tradeoff]].
