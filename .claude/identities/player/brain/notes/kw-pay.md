**Pay** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.31).

Pay is a discrete effect. To pay an asset-cost, the player spends assets of the specified type and amount.[1.14.2] 8.5.31a Pay effects are optional and the affected player can refuse to pay the asset-cost.
