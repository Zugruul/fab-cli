Swarming attacks off **Luminaris**-animated **Spectral Shield** tokens is powerful, but each token-attack is fragile as a chain link: kill the token before its Damage Step (any damage source that can hit a permanent) and the whole attack evaporates with no damage dealt, per the CR's own Luminaris/Spectral Shield example under 7.7.2c. If you're the Luminaris player, sequence your own token-attacks so you're not leaving a lone activated Spectral Shield exposed to a cheap removal/damage instant before it connects — attack with backup queued (other attacks already in the queue) so even if one token-attack gets sniped pre-damage, the chain doesn't fully close and your other queued attacks still resolve.
