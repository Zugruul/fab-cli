Static-ability flavors you'll read on cards (CR 5.4): ADDITIONAL cost ("As an additional cost to play this…") vs ALTERNATIVE cost ("You may X rather than pay…") — alternatives replace the base cost but do NOT dodge additional costs or cost increases (5.4.4b). "When you do, …" = triggers immediately on paying (5.4.4c). Property-statics (Mutated Mass) work everywhere, even outside games (5.4.5a). While-statics work from private zones only when the condition names that zone — Heave works face-down in hand ("While this is in your hand"), Blood Debt does NOT work face-down in banish because banish is public by default (5.4.7a). Hidden triggered abilities (The Librarian face-down in arsenal): optional trigger; source flips public while the trigger resolves, then re-hides (5.4.7b). Meta-statics losing mid-game doesn't retro-invalidate your deck (Shiyana, 5.4.3a). Links: [[abilities-and-functionality]], [[arsenal]], [[playing-a-card-nine-steps]].
