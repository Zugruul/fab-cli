Classic Constructed (CC) is the flagship 1v1 constructed format (fabtcg.com/gameplay-formats/classic-constructed/). Rules: 1 non-young (adult) hero; an 80-card card-pool including weapons/equipment; deck a minimum of 60 cards; up to 3 copies of each unique card; best-of-1, 55 min rounds. A card is legal in your pool only if its class/talents are a subset of your hero's (generic cards go in any deck). "Unique" = distinct name AND color, so red vs blue copies count separately ([[pitch-values-and-color]]). CC is the deep, high-variety format most competitive events use. Current bans/restrictions are NOT memorized — see [[card-legality-pointer]]. Contrast the young-hero formats [[format-silver-age]] and [[format-blitz]]; the eternal variant is [[format-living-legend]]. Hub: [[player-brain-map]].
