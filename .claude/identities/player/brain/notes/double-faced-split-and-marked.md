Advanced card shapes (CR 9): FLIP-cards (figment/invocation/construct front) — front active outside arena; construct/invocation enters arena back-face active; destroyed → front again (Nitro Mechanoid, 9.1.3b). TWIN-cards — BOTH faces active outside arena/stack (both abilities live from inventory, Levia Redeemed, 9.1.4a); one face active in play. TRANSCEND-cards — back face becomes active on transcend and STAYS active for the rest of the game, even through resets (Stir the Pot → Inner Chi forever, 9.1.5b). SPLIT-cards (Null||Shock) — one card with ALL properties of both sides until put on the stack, where you pick a side (9.2.2-3); deck legality needs BOTH sides' supertypes (9.2.2a); naming one side (Chains of Eminence "Shock") stops pitching/defending with the whole card but the other side can still be PLAYED (9.2.2b example — Regrowth playable). Meld plays both sides ([[ability-keywords-complete]]). MARKED (9.3): a hero condition; cleared when HIT by an opponent-controlled source (part of the hit event, not a trigger); re-marking a marked hero does nothing. Living Legend status = whatever https://fabtcg.com/living-legend/ says — never memorize ([[card-legality-pointer]]). Links: [[keywords-index]], [[hit-events-and-what-counts]].
