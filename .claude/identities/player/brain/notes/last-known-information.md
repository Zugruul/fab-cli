Last known information (LKI): when an object ceases to exist, rules/effects that need it use a snapshot from immediately before it vanished (CR 1.2.3) — state, counters, parameters, history, applied effects (1.2.3b). LKI is immutable (1.2.3c: meeting Luminaris' condition AFTER the attack left the chain does not grant go again) and is not a targetable object (1.2.3d). Player value: hit-triggers still pay off even if the attack card left the chain (Endless Arrow returns to hand and its chain link still grants the action point, 1.2.3a).
