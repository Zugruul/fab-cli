Fusion costs are cheaper than they look: revealing an Ice card to fuse **Aether Icevein** doesn't remove it from your hand (CR 8.3.17), so that same card stays available to play, pitch, or fuse a SECOND Fusion card later the same turn. When sequencing a turn with multiple Fusion cards of the same element, you often only need to hold ONE qualifying card in hand and keep re-revealing it across each Fusion play — don't overload your hand with redundant Ice cards "just in case" each Fusion needs its own; one Ice card can fuel every Fusion cost you pay that turn as long as it stays in hand.
