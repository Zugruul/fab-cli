Continuous-effect timing a player must know (CR 6.2): no stated duration + no condition = ends at end of THIS turn (6.2.2a — Tempest Aurora doesn't carry over). "Next attack…" buffs LOCK onto the first qualifying object and stick even if its properties later change (Cut Deep stays on a Kiss of Death that stops being a dagger, 6.2.2c) — and X in such effects is fixed when it first applies (Tear Limb from Limb reads base {p} at apply-time, 6.2.2b). Layer-effects have fixed durations; static-effects live while their ability is functional and their X updates live (6.2.3b). Conditional statics blink on/off with the condition (Luminaris go-again exists only while a yellow is in pitch, 6.2.3d — time your pitches!). Rule-modifying effects apply before object-modifying ones: Hypothermia's "can't gain go again" beats any go-again grant, but does NOT strip printed go again (6.3.1, 6.3.7).
