Don't count on **Clash of Agility**'s payoff every time you defend with it — if both players happen to reveal a card with no power stat (auras, instants, items on top of deck), the clash has NO WINNER (CR 8.5.45c) and nobody gets the Agility token. Deckbuilding-wise, running a higher density of attack-type (power-bearing) cards in your deck increases the odds YOUR top card has power when a clash triggers, tilting these "coin flip" clash cards more consistently in your favor over a match.
