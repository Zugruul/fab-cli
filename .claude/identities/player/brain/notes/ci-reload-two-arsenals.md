Running **New Horizon** for the extra arsenal zone? Know that it actively works against reload payoffs like **Bolt'n' Shot** — CR 8.5.23a requires ALL of your arsenal zones to be empty before reload can fire, so a second (even useful, occupied) arsenal zone can silently deny your reload triggers turn after turn. If your gameplan leans on reload-matters synergies, don't run New Horizon in the same deck unless you're disciplined about keeping BOTH arsenal zones clear before your reload-generating attacks resolve — otherwise you're paying New Horizon's cost without getting the reload upside you built around.
