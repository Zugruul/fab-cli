**Ally** — subtype keyword (CR 8.2.8).

8.2.8a If an ally permanent ceases to exist, it is considered to have died.[2.5.3g] 8.2.8b During the End Phase, an ally's life total is reset to its base life.[4.4] 8.2.8c If an ally is attacking, the controlling player and their hero are considered an attacking hero for that chain link.[7.2.3a] 8.2.8d If an ally is the target of an attack, the controlling player and their hero are considered a defending hero for that chain link, but the player cannot declare defending cards or play or activate defense reaction cards or abilities during the reaction step of combat.[7.2.3b] 8.2.8e If an ally deals damage, the controlling player and their hero are not considered to have dealt damage. 8.2.8f If an ally is dealt damage, the controlling player and their hero are not considered to have been dealt damage.
