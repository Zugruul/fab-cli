15 zone types (CR 3.0.1). PRIVATE zones: arsenal, deck, hand (3.0.4b) — everything else public. The ARENA = arms+chest+head+legs+hero+weapon×2+combat chain+permanent zones only (3.1.1) — graveyard, banished, pitch, stack, deck, hand, arsenal are NOT in the arena. You can look at private objects you own EXCEPT your deck (3.0.3a, 3.7.4). Equipment zones hold one card each, matched by subtype; 2H weapons occupy both weapon zones (3.16.2a). Arsenal: one deck-card; effects that would over-fill it FAIL (3.3.2a). Pitch zone is PUBLIC — opponents see and count what you pitched; play around it and track theirs ([[second-cycle-awareness]]). Stack + queue: attacks waiting in the queue count as "on the stack" for functionality/targeting (3.15.7f). Inventory is NOT a zone — it's your unselected card-pool, private, consulted for matchup swaps (4.1.6). Links: [[arsenal]], [[object-resets-and-zone-moves]].
