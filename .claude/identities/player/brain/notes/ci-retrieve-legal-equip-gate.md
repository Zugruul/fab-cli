Retrieve effects like **Pick Up the Point**'s dagger-from-graveyard are risk-free to attempt — CR 8.5.51a guarantees you're never asked to pay {r} unless the retrieved card can legally be equipped right then (an open matching zone). So there's no reason to hold back from checking retrieve "just in case it wastes resources" — if your weapon zone is already full, the game simply won't offer you the option, no {r} lost. Practical deckbuilding note: if you want retrieve to actually connect a graveyard dagger back into play, make sure you're not running so many equipped weapons/off-hands that your relevant zone stays permanently full — leave room for the payoff.
