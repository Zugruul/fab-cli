The arsenal ([[arsenal]]) is a one-card bridge across the end-of-turn redraw: a card you cannot use this turn need not be wasted ([[intellect-and-hand-refill]]) — arsenal it and it is available next turn ON TOP of your fresh hand, effectively giving you a 5th card of selection. Common uses: bank a defense reaction to play from arsenal mid-combat on the opponent's turn ([[attack-and-defense-reactions]]); set up a combo/reprise piece so next turn opens explosively ([[keyword-attack-labels]]); hold a specific answer. Because you arsenal in the End Phase before drawing (CR 4.4.3b; [[turn-structure-phases]]), you decide with full knowledge of the turn's leftovers. Good arsenal management is a hallmark of strong play and feeds [[tempo-and-go-again-chains]].
