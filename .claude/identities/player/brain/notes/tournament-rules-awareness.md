Sanctioned FAB play is governed by the Tournament Rules and Policy (TRP), separate from the Comprehensive Rules — the TRP can supersede a game rule in a tournament (CR 1.0.1b), while the CR governs how the game itself works. For a player, light awareness is enough: match structure is best-of-1 with round timers that vary by format (55 min CC/LL, 35 min Blitz/SAGE — see the format notes); decks/card-pools must be registered; there are procedures for shuffling/presenting (CR 4.1.7) and end-of-round extra turns when time is called ([[format-living-legend]] page describes the +1 turn rule). Full details: https://fabtcg.com/rules-and-policy-center/ . Card legality within a tournament is still the live policy ([[card-legality-pointer]]). Penalties are handled by judges, not players ([[penalty-guidelines-awareness]]). Hub: [[player-brain-map]].
