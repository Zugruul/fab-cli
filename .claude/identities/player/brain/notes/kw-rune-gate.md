**Rune Gate** — ability keyword (CR 8.3.27).

Rune Gate is a play-static ability that means "If you control Runechants equal to or greater than this's {r} cost, you may play it from your banished zone without paying its {r} cost." 8.3.27a If a player plays a card from their banished zone using rune gate, they are considered to have rune gated, and the card is considered to be rune gated.
