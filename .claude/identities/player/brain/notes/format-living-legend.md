Living Legend (LL) is an eternal constructed format built on Classic Constructed (fabtcg.com/gameplay-formats/living-legend/): 1 non-young hero, 80-card pool, 60-card minimum deck, up to 3 copies unique, best-of-1 55 min — same construction as [[format-classic-constructed]]. Its point is that heroes and signature weapons which have RETIRED from CC by reaching Living Legend status are playable here, so old/powerful heroes find continued play. The card pool and legality are governed by the LL rotation mechanism ([[living-legend-rotation-system]]) and the live policy ([[card-legality-pointer]]) — do not memorize which heroes are LL. Essence and Legendary still shape deckbuilding. Hub: [[player-brain-map]].
