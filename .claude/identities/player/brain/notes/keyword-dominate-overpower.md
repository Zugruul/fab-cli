Two keywords that SUPPRESS blocking. Dominate: "This can't be defended by more than one card from hand" (CR 8.3.4) — so a dominated attack can be blocked by at most one hand card (plus equipment), and once one hand card is blocking, no defense reactions from hand either (CR 8.3.4b). Overpower: "This can't be defended by more than one action card" (CR 8.3.22). Both force damage through against defenders who wanted to gang-block, and pair naturally with big single attacks and on-hit effects ([[hit-determination-and-damage]]). As the defender, recognize these on the attack before planning blocks ([[block-vs-take-tradeoff]], [[defending-from-hand]]). Neither is retroactive — gaining the keyword after blocks are declared removes nothing (CR 8.3.4c, 8.3.22b). Related pressure keyword: [[keyword-piercing]]. Verify wording: [[card-corpus-and-search]].
