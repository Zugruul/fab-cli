When you stop attacking (no more attacks, all players pass) the combat chain closes (CR 7.6.4, 7.7). At close: equipped permanents and weapons return to their zones (CR 7.7.5) — so your weapon comes back ([[weapon-attacks]]) — and all other cards on the chain (spent attacks, blocks, reactions) go to the graveyard (learn-to-play transcript; CR 7.7.6). Effects lasting "this combat chain" end (CR 7.7.7). Strategic points: cards you blocked with are gone to graveyard, not recycled through your deck like pitched cards ([[second-cycle-awareness]]) — blocking permanently spends the card. The chain closing also ends the window for chain-link-count effects, so all your go-again sequencing has to happen within one open chain. See [[combat-chain-and-chain-links]], [[tempo-and-go-again-chains]].
