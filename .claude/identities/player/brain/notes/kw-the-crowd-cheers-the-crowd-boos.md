**The Crowd Cheers / The Crowd Boos** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.57).

The crowd cheers/boos is a discrete effect. The player that is cheered/booed is considered to be cheered/booed. 8.5.57a If a player is cheered and booed, the are considered both cheered and booed. The effects do not cancel each other out.
