End-of-turn procedure runs in EXACT order (CR 4.4.3, triggers processed between steps): (a) allies' life resets to base; (b) turn-player MAY arsenal one hand card into an EMPTY arsenal zone; (c) each player bottoms their pitch zone in an order they choose — the order is hidden information (pitch-stacking is legal and invisible, 4.4.3c); (d) turn-player untaps their permanents; (e) ALL players lose all action + resource points (floating resources vanish); (f) turn-player refills to intellect (first turn of game: everyone refills — see [[first-turn-rule]]); no draw if already at/above intellect. Then "this turn"/"until end of turn" effects end and the next player's Start Phase begins (4.4.4). Player takeaway: arsenal BEFORE refill, so arsenal-then-draw is card-neutral; leftover resources can't be banked.
