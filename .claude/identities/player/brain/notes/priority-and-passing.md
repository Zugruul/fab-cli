Priority = who may act (CR 1.11). Only ONE active player at a time; priority exists only in the Action Phase (never during the Close Step) (1.11.3). Turn-player gets priority at action-phase start, during most combat steps, and after each layer resolves. If everyone passes in succession: top of stack resolves; if the stack is empty, the phase/step ends (1.11.4a). You regain priority after playing/activating (1.11.5) — so you can chain your own responses. Before anyone gets priority, game-state actions run automatically: deaths first, 0-life objects cleared, pending triggers go on the stack, chain-close check (1.10.2). Off-turn constraint: you can NEVER gain action points outside your own action phase — an instant-played "go again"/"gain 1 action point" does nothing for the defender (1.13.2b).
