If you're building around "whenever you gain control of X" payoffs, note that Steal effects (e.g. **Mutiny on the Battalion Barque**) count toward them — Steal is defined in the CR as gaining control (CR 8.5.54), so a generic gain-control synergy piece triggers off your steal cards too, letting you count both effect types toward the same payoff density in your deck. But the reverse doesn't hold: don't expect a "steal"-specific payoff to trigger off a plain gain-control card like **Kyloria** — that only counts as gaining control, not as having stolen, so keep those two card pools distinct when the payoff explicitly says "steal."
