Don't assume freezing an opponent's arsenal (e.g. with **Cold Snap**) shuts down their **Stadium Security**-style ambush blocker — freeze (CR 8.5.34) only stops PLAYING the card and activating its abilities, not defending with it. A frozen ambush card in arsenal is still a live block. If you're trying to strip away a surprise arsenal blocker, freeze is the wrong tool; look for effects that actually remove/tap/banish the card from arsenal, or that specifically say cards can't defend. Conversely, if YOU have an ambush attacker in arsenal and it gets frozen, don't panic — you can still flip it in to defend, you just can't play it as your turn's attack while frozen.
