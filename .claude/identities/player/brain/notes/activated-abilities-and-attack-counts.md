Activated abilities read "[LIMIT?] [TYPE] -- [COST]: [ABILITIES] [CONDITION?]" (CR 5.2.1); no LIMIT = activate freely if you can pay. Activation follows the same steps as playing a card (5.2.2b) and needs priority + functional ability + you control the source. "Attack an additional time with target sword" = one extra activation past LIMIT (Twinning Blade, 5.2.3c). Additional-activation effects are ADDITIVE and shared across the object's abilities (Tri-shot on a two-ability bow: split 2 extra any way, 5.2.3d); but "may attack twice" SETS a limit — applying it twice doesn't give four attacks (Blood on Her Hands, 5.2.3e). Activating from a private zone (hand/arsenal) makes the source public until resolution — this reveal triggers nothing (5.2.4b).
