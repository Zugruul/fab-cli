Your hero starts with a weapon (and equipment) in play (CR 4.1; learn-to-play transcript). A weapon attack is an activated attack ability (the "Attack" keyword, CR 8.3.1): activating it puts an attack-proxy on the stack, and when it resolves the weapon moves onto the combat chain as a chain link (CR 7.2.2b). Weapon attacks usually cost an action point (they are action-type activations) and often have a resource cost. Unlike attack action cards, the weapon RETURNS to your weapon zone when the chain closes instead of going to the graveyard (CR 7.7.5; transcript: "Edge of Autumn returns to the weapon zone"). This makes weapons a repeatable, no-card-cost source of pressure every turn — valuable when your hand is better spent pitching or defending.
