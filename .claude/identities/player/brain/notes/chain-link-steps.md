Each chain link resolves through steps: Layer, Attack, Defend, Reaction, Damage, Resolution (CR 7.0.1), then the Close Step ends combat (CR 7.7). The learn-to-play transcript condenses this to the four player-facing beats: (1) attacker plays an attack; (2) defender declares any number of blocking cards from hand — free, all declared simultaneously in the Defend Step (CR 7.3); (3) the Reaction window, where attacker plays attack reactions and defender plays defense reactions (CR 7.4); (4) Damage — compare attack power to total defense (CR 7.5). If the attack has go again, the attacker gets an action point in the Resolution Step and may attack again, adding chain link N+1 (CR 7.6.2).
