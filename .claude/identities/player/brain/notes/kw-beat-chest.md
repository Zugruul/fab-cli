**Beat Chest** — ability keyword (CR 8.3.33).

Beat Chest is a play-static ability that means "As an additional cost to play this, you may discard a card with 6 or more {p}." 8.3.33a If a player pays the additional cost to play a card with beat chest, the player is considered to have beaten chest. 8.3.33b A player cannot beat chest if they cannot pay the additional cost of discarding a card with 6 or more {p}.
