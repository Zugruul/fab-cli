Three ability categories (CR 1.7.3): ACTIVATED (you pay, creates a layer), RESOLUTION (fires when the layer resolves), STATIC (just generates effects). An ability is functional only while its source is public AND in the arena (1.7.4) — with exceptions that matter in play: a DEFENDING non-permanent card's abilities are OFF by default (1.7.4a — only abilities that say they work while defending, e.g. Rally the Rearguard, or while-statics like Yinti Yanti); hand-activated abilities work from hand when the cost demands it (Mighty Windup, 1.7.4b); Specialization/deck-building limits are meta-static and work outside the game (1.7.4d); "play this from banished" is play-static (1.7.4e). MODAL abilities ("choose 2;"): modes declared when the card goes on the stack, distinct modes unless stated (1.7.5a-b). Links: [[layers-and-stack]], [[defending-from-hand]], [[card-anatomy]].
