Limited + multiplayer specs a player needs (TRP 8-9): SEALED and DRAFT decks are EXACTLY 30 cards, young hero from the set, unlimited copies, basics free from the set's pool. Draft: pods of 8, 3 packs passed left→right→left; absolutely no table-talk/signaling; picks are final; you may only review between packs. UPF: young hero, 40-card deck exactly, max 2 copies, target only your immediate left/right neighbors unless an effect says "any hero" ("any target" wording does NOT unlock the table — big deckbuilding consideration). Team play: talk freely with teammates mid-match (except during team drafts), but the seated player decides.
