In any multi-hero format, **Alpha Rampage**'s "intimidate" (no explicit target printed) still requires YOU to pick which opposing hero eats the random-hand-banish (CR 8.5.10b) — it is not locked to whichever hero you're attacking. Use that choice for value: target whichever opponent has the highest-impact hand (most cards, or a known bomb) rather than defaulting to your attack's target, since the temporary banish (returns at your end phase) is really just a one-turn tempo/hand-disruption tool, best aimed at whoever benefits your board state most to disrupt.
