If you're running multiple Arcanite-named Spellvoid pieces (e.g. **Arcanite Fortress**) alongside a flat-value Spellvoid source like **Claw of Vynserakai**, sequence your preventions deliberately against a single arcane damage event: destroy the FIXED-value Spellvoid source first, and destroy any Arcanite-named piece whose X you still need LAST, since destroying one Arcanite-named equipment shrinks the live count (and thus X) for every other Arcanite-named piece still on the field (CR 8.3.15 — Spellvoid X recalculates continuously off "equipment you control with Arcanite in their name"). Building an Arcanite-name subtheme rewards keeping more pieces alive longer, not burning them in the wrong order under damage.
