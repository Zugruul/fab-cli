Ultimate Pit Fight (UPF) is the casual MULTIPLAYER format for 3+ players (fabtcg.com/gameplay-formats/ultimate-pit-fight-multi-player/): 1 hero, every card legal (including special promos), politics/alliances/backstabbing encouraged, rules flexible per playgroup. It can be layered on any base format but expands to multiple players — so turn order is clockwise among all players and attacks pick a target. The core combat and resource rules still hold, but table dynamics (who to attack, when to ally) dominate. Not the competitive 1v1 experience. Distinct from the 1v1 constructed formats [[format-classic-constructed]] / [[format-silver-age]]. Hub: [[player-brain-map]].
