**Add (defend)** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.32).

Add is a discrete effect. To add a card to a chain link as a defending card, move it from its current zone onto that chain link as a defending card.[7.0.5] 8.5.32a If an effect would prevent a card from defending on the specified chain link, the defend effect fails and the card is not moved.
