**Crush** — label keyword (CR 8.4.2).

Crush is a label for a triggered-static ability typically written as "Crush - When this deals 4 or more damage, [EFFECTS]." 8.4.2a The Crush ability is conditional on an event that deals damage, not just a hit-event.[7.5.5]
