**Attack** — subtype keyword (CR 8.2.3).

8.2.3a An attack card is considered an attack when on the stack or when it is on the combat chain as an attacking card.[1.4.2] 8.2.3b When an attack card is played, the combat chain opens (if it is closed) and the layer step of combat begins.[7.1]
