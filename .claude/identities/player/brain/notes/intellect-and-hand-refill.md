Your hero's intellect is your hand size. At the end of your turn you draw back up to intellect (most heroes 4) (learn-to-play transcript; CR 4.4.3f). The strategic consequence: cards left unused in hand at end of turn are "wasted" value, because you refill to the same number regardless. So the transcript's advice — "use all the cards in your hand each turn to maximize the benefit of this redraw" — is the single most important beginner principle. This is why FAB is about squeezing maximum value out of each 4-card hand, not stockpiling. It directly motivates [[hand-value-card-advantage]], [[pitch-curve-planning]], and using leftover cards on defense (see [[block-vs-take-tradeoff]]). Refill happens in the End Phase after pitch and arsenal steps ([[turn-structure-phases]]).
