Trigger facts that change lines (CR 6.6): triggers must EXIST before their event — nothing triggers retroactively (6.6.5a). "The first time each turn…" ordinals count from the duration's start even before the card arrived — boost first, THEN play the permanent and its first-boost trigger is already spent (6.6.5d). Prevented triggers still consume their limit (Tripwire Trap eats Katsu's once-per-turn, 6.6.5f). A trigger needing targets with none legal simply ceases — never hits the stack (6.6.6a). YOU order your own simultaneous triggers (6.6.6b — sequence gain-{r} before pay-{r}). Delayed triggers ("the next time you play…") persist until they fire or their duration ends (6.6.3a). State-triggers re-check at resolution and fizzle if the state changed ([[layer-resolution-order]] phantasm). Links: [[events-and-multi-events]], [[priority-and-passing]].
