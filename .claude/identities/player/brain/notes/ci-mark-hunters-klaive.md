**Hunter's Klaive** reliably keeps your opponent marked turn over turn — each hit clears any pre-existing mark as part of the hit itself (CR 9.3.3), then Klaive's own "when this hits, mark them" trigger reapplies it fresh right after. You don't need to worry about "wasting" a mark by hitting an already-marked hero with Klaive; the reapplication always lands. The real way to make an opponent lose their marked status against you is to simply NOT hit them with anything for a full turn cycle, or have THEM get hit by someone else's attack after your last hit and before you swing again — any opposing-controlled hit clears it (9.3.2b), including from a teammate's attack in team formats.
