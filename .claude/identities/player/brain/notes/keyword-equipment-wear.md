Equipment blocks are free and repeatable, but several keywords wear equipment down as it defends. Battleworn: "When the combat chain closes, if this defended, put a -1{d} counter on it" (CR 8.3.2). Blade Break: "...if this defended, destroy it" (CR 8.3.3) — one-and-done blocks, often on weapons/off-hands. Temper: "...put a -1{d} counter on it, then destroy it if it has zero {d}" (CR 8.3.10). Guardwell strips all its defense at once (CR 8.3.34). Implication for play: equipment with these can only soak a limited number of hits, so sequence which equipment you block with and when ([[block-vs-take-tradeoff]]). Attackers exploit this alongside [[keyword-piercing]]. Confirm each piece's keyword and defense in [[card-corpus-and-search]].
