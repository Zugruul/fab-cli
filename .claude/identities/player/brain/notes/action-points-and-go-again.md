At the start of your Action Phase you get exactly 1 action point (CR 4.3.2). Playing an action card or activating an action ability costs 1 action point (CR 8.1.1c), so by default you get ONE action per turn. The keyword "go again" grants 1 action point when its card resolves (CR 8.3.5), effectively letting you keep going. Non-turn cost: you cannot gain action points outside your own action phase (CR 1.13.2b) — this is why defending and reactions do not need action points. Chaining go-again cards is how FAB turns become multi-card sequences — see [[keyword-go-again]] and [[tempo-and-go-again-chains]]. Instants and reactions do NOT cost an action point (CR 8.1.6a, 8.1.1d). Non-attack actions without go again end your turn after resolving (learn-to-play transcript).
