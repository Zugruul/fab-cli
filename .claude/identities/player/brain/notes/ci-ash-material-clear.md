When you transform/attach an **Ash** token under one of your attacks to grant it phantasm, remember the protection ends the instant the top-card leaves the arena — Ash doesn't linger as a separate object you can salvage or re-use (CR 3.0.14e: sub-cards clear in the same event as their top-card's destruction). Don't plan around "the Ash survives in graveyard and I recur it separately" — it's gone the moment its host is gone, same event, no extra value to extract. Sequence Material-granting tokens as a one-shot property attached to the specific permanent you're protecting that turn, not as a durable resource.
