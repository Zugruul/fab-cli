The Penalty Guidelines (PPG) govern how JUDGES assign penalties for infractions — it exists so a player can recognize when a penalty is being MIS-applied, NOT so a player can rules-lawyer an opponent. As a player: call a judge for disputes, play honestly, and do not try to weaponize procedural penalties. If you believe a ruling or penalty was applied incorrectly, the correct move is calm escalation to a higher judge, not arguing at the table.
