Prevention effects (CR 6.4.10) are replacement effects on damage events. Two shapes: FIXED-prevention ("the next time you would be dealt damage, prevent X") — applies to ONE event; leftover prevention evaporates (6.4.10i). SHIELDING-prevention ("prevent the next X damage… this turn") — a pool that persists across multiple events until drained (6.4.10j, Bone Head Barrier). Unstated amount = the whole event (Feign Death, 6.4.10b); unstated type = all damage types (6.4.10c). Against unpreventable damage: the effect still "applies" — a fixed effect like Enchanting Melody is USED UP (and still destroyed) preventing nothing, but a shield's pool survives (6.4.10h). Defense planning: prefer shields into multi-hit turns, fixed prevention into single big hits, and never waste fixed prevention into unpreventable damage.
