Playing against a piercing attack like **Drill Shot** (with an aim counter live)? Don't assume double-blocking with an equipment PLUS a hand card dodges the piercing bonus — piercing (CR 8.3.23) only checks whether an equipment is AMONG the defenders, not whether it's the sole defender. Stacking a hand card alongside your equipment block still eats the +1{p}. If you want to avoid piercing entirely, you need to defend with zero equipment cards on that chain link (all-hand-card block, or take it to the face) — mixing in extra defenders doesn't help.
