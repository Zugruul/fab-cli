The 7 combat steps in CR precision (deepens [[chain-link-steps]]): LAYER (attack sits on the stack — respond NOW with instants before it becomes attacking) → ATTACK (attack moves from queue to chain; "when this attacks" triggers fire; attacking/defending hero labels lock, 7.2.3) → DEFEND (defenders declared from hand + equipment, FREE, as one multi-event in an order YOU choose — sequencing matters for Flic Flak-style "next card you defend" effects, 7.3.2c-d) → REACTION (attack reactions for attacker, defense reactions for defender; a DR resolving becomes a defending card — a second DR under dominate FAILS to resolve, 7.4.2d) → DAMAGE ({p} vs total {d}; excess = hit-event) → RESOLUTION (chain-link-resolve triggers; go again grants its action point HERE, 7.6.2; attacker may launch the next attack) → CLOSE (no priority; everything clears; "this combat chain" effects end). While the chain is open you cannot play ACTIONS except attacks during Resolution — instants always fine with priority (7.0.1a).
