Label keywords group attack conditionals (CR 8.4). Combo: "If [named card] was the last attack this chain, [effect]" (CR 8.4.1) — rewards sequencing specific cards in order on the chain ([[combat-chain-and-chain-links]]). Reprise: "If the defending hero defended with a card from hand this chain link, [effect]" (CR 8.4.3) — rewards you when the opponent blocks from hand, punishing them either way. Crush: "When this deals 4 or more damage, [effect]" (CR 8.4.2) — rewards pushing damage through, so it pairs with blocking-suppression ([[keyword-dominate-overpower]]). Surge: "If this deals N damage, [effect]" (CR 8.4.8); Rupture rewards playing at chain link 4+ (CR 8.4.6). These labels are why sequencing and go-again chains ([[tempo-and-go-again-chains]]) matter — order and chain position unlock extra value. Verify exact names/effects: [[card-corpus-and-search]].
