**Hero** — type keyword (CR 8.1.5).

A hero card is a hero-card.[1.3.2a] 8.1.5a A player starts the game with their hero card as a permanent in their hero zone.[4.1] 8.1.5b A hero card is separate from, and cannot be included in a player's, card-pool.[1.1.3]
