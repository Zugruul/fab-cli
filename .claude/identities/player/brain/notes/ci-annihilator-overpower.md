**Annihilator Engine** only reaches overpower at 3+ equipped Evos. Deckbuilding/sequencing takeaway: front-load your Evo equips BEFORE you commit to attacking with Annihilator Engine, not after — because overpower (CR 8.3.22a) only blocks a SECOND action card from being added to defend going forward; it can't undo an action-card defender your opponent already committed earlier in the same chain link (CR 8.3.22b, same rule that protects dominate). If you're below the Evo threshold when the opponent adds their second action-card defender, gaining overpower a beat later buys you nothing on that swing — so play your Evo pieces first, then attack, to guarantee the restriction is live at the actual defend decision.
