Functional subtypes (CR 8.2): (1H)/(2H) — one/two weapon zones (a 2H occupies either zone, 8.2.2c). ATTACK — opens the chain when played. AURA/ITEM/ASH/FIGMENT/AFFLICTION — resolve into the arena as permanents (not when defending); AFFLICTION enters under a chosen OPPONENT's control (8.2.11c). LANDMARK — entering clears all other landmarks (8.2.9b, highlander rule). ARROW — playable only from arsenal and only with a bow (8.2.6a). ALLY — dies like a living object, life resets each End Phase, attacks/defends as pseudo-hero but its controller can't declare defenders or DRs for it, and its damage isn't "your hero's" damage in either direction (8.2.8d-f). OFF-HAND/QUIVER — weapon-zone gear, max one each; a quiver can share a 2H bow's unoccupied zone (8.2.15a). INVOCATION/CONSTRUCT — enter back-face active (double-faced, 8.2.13-14). Trap is no longer functional (8.2.7). Links: [[names-monikers-classes-talents]], [[zones-and-visibility]].
