**Defense Reaction** — type keyword (CR 8.1.3).

A defense reaction card is a deck-card.[1.3.2c] 8.1.3a A defense reaction card/activated ability can only be played/activated by a player who controls a hero as an attack-target during the Reaction Step of combat.[7.4] 8.1.3b When a defense reaction card resolves as a layer on the stack, it becomes a defending card on the active chain link.[7.0.5] 8.1.3c A defense reaction card/activated ability is considered to be a reaction card/ability.
