**(2H)** — subtype keyword (CR 8.2.2).

8.2.2a A (2H) object considered to be a two-hander. 8.2.2b A two-hander permanent must be equipped to two of a player's weapon zones. A two-hander cannot be equipped if the player does not have two empty weapon zones. 8.2.2c A two-hander occupies either of the two weapon zones it is equipped to, but not both.
