**Attack (with)** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.38).

Attack (with) is a targeted discrete effect. To attack a target with a card, the card is added to the queue as an attack. Only attackable objects may be targeted.[1.4.5a] 8.5.38a A layer on the stack with a resolution ability that can generate an attack (with) effect is an attack-layer.[1.4.4] 8.5.38b If the card does not have the attack subtype, instead an attack-proxy is put into the queue with the card as its attack-source. 8.5.38c If the card doesn't exist, nothing is put into the queue and the effect fails.
