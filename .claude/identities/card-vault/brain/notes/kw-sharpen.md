**Sharpen** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.58).

Sharpen is a discrete effect and delayed-triggered effect. To sharpen a card, put a +1{p} counter on it, and then at the beginning of the end phase, remove all +1{p} counters from it. 8.5.58a The player is considered to sharpened, and the card is considered to be sharpened.
