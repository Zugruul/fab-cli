**Shuffle** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.20).

Shuffle is a discrete effect. To shuffle a zone, put the cards in that zone in a sufficiently random order such that no one knows their order. 8.5.20a If there are no cards in the zone to shuffle, the zone is still considered shuffled. 8.5.20b If the effect specifies to shuffle cards into a zone, put the cards into the zone then shuffle that zone. 8.5.20c If the effect does not specify what zone to shuffle, the player shuffles their own deck.
