**Landmark** — subtype keyword (CR 8.2.9).

8.2.9a When a landmark resolves as a layer on the stack, it enters the arena. 8.2.9b When a landmark enters the arena, it becomes a permanent and all other landmark permanents are cleared, except when the landmark is added as a defending card to a chain link.[3.0.12]
