**Opt** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.22).

Opt is a discrete effect. To opt N, the player looks at the top N cards of their deck, then puts any number of those cards on the top or bottom of their deck in any order. 8.5.22a If the deck has less than N cards, the player will look at all cards in their deck and put them back in any order. 8.5.22b If the deck has no cards in it, the opt effect fails.
