**Modular** — ability keyword (CR 8.3.30).

Modular is a static ability that means "This may be equipped to any of your equipment zones. It has the subtype of the zone it's equipped to." 8.3.30a The equipment zones are Arms, Chest, Head, and Legs. 8.3.30b A card can only be equipped to its owner's equipment zones with Modular. 8.3.30c A card with modular does not have any of the equipment subtypes until it is equipped to a zone.
