**Blood Debt** — ability keyword (CR 8.3.11).

Blood Debt is a triggered-static ability. Blood debt means "While this is in your banished zone, at the beginning of your end phase, lose 1{h}." 8.3.11a Blood debt only triggers if its source is public in the banished zone at the beginning of the owner's end phase.
