**Retrieve** — effect keyword (CR 8.5.51).

Retrieve is a discrete effect. To retrieve a card, the player may pay {r} to equip the card. 8.5.51a The card must exist and be able to be equipped for the player to pay {r}. If the player cannot equip the card, they cannot choose to pay {r}.
