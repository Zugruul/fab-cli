**Equipment** — type keyword (CR 8.1.4).

An equipment card (without any other types) is an arena-card.[1.3.2d] 8.1.4a As an arena-card, a player may equip an equipment card from their card-pool during the start-of-game procedure to its respective zone.[4.1] 8.1.4b An equipment permanent may be declared as a defending card during the Defend Step of combat.[7.3.2]
