**Ephemeral** — ability keyword (CR 8.3.21).

Ephemeral is a meta-static ability and a static ability that respectively mean "You can't start the game with this in your deck." and "If this would be put into a graveyard from anywhere, instead it ceases to exist." 8.3.21a A player cannot include a card with Ephemeral in their card-pool.[1.1.3] 8.3.21b A card that ceases to exist from Ephemeral is removed from the game. A card that is removed from the game has no further interaction with the rules and effects in the game.
