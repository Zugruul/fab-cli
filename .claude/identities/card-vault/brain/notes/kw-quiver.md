**Quiver** — subtype keyword (CR 8.2.15).

8.2.15a A quiver permanent must be equipped to a player's weapon zone. A quiver may be equipped to, and occupy, a weapon zone that a two-hander bow is already equipped to but does not occupy; otherwise, a quiver cannot be equipped if the player does not have an empty weapon zone. 8.2.15b A player cannot equip more than one quiver.
