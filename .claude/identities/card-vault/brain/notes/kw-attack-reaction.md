**Attack Reaction** — type keyword (CR 8.1.2).

An attack reaction card is a deck-card.[1.3.2c] 8.1.2a An attack reaction card/activated ability can only be played/activated by a player who controls the attack during the Reaction Step of combat.[7.4] 8.1.2b When an attack reaction card resolves as a layer on the stack, it is cleared.[3.0.12] 8.1.2c An attack reaction card/activated ability is considered to be a reaction card/ability.
